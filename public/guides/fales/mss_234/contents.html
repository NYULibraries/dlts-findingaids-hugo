Series I: The Explorers Club Annual Dinner Programs, 1973-2002

Extent

0.25 Linear Feet in 1 half manuscript box

Scope and Content

This series consists of programs from the majority of Explorers Club Annual Dinners held from 1973 to 2002 (missing years are 1974, 1977, 1977, 1978, 1989, 1997, and 2001). The programs include brief biographies of recipients of the Explorers Medal, the Edward C. Sweeney Medal, and any additional honorees, a menu (usually consisting of exotic foods), further biographies of speakers from the event, and a listing of current officers and board members of the Club. Some programs include additional materials such as tickets or other publicity materials.
