Series I: Correspondence, Manuscripts, Covers, and Publications

Scope and Contents note

The C: A Journal of PoetryArchive is a collection of correspondence and manuscripts submitted to the editor, Ted Berrigan. Collection highlights include letters and manuscripts written by John Ashbery, Marianne Moore, James Schuyler and William Saroyan.

Correspondence and manuscripts - "A-L" including materials by John Ashbery, Barbara Guest, Kenneth Koch, and Joseph Le Sueur., undated
