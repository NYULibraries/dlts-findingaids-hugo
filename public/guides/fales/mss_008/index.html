Scope and Content Note

The C: A Journal of Poetry Archive contains correspondence and manuscripts submitted to the editor, Ted Berrigan. Collection highlights include correspondence and manuscripts written by John Ashbery, Marianne Moore, and James Schuyler. The collection also includes issues of the magazine.
