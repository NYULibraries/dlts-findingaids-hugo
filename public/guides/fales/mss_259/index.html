Abstract

The Curt Royston Papers consists of reviews, promotional matierals, transparencies, film, and video recordings relating to Royston's career as a filmmaker. Materials date from 1982 to 2000.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Donated by Curt Royston in 2009 and 2014. The accession numbers associated with these gifts are 2009.259 and 2014.259.

Biographical Note

Curt Royston is best known for his proficiency in a variety of media: painting, sculpture, photography, film, and video, and often in combination. His work has been shown extensively throughout the United States and Europe including a 1987 one-man exhibition at the Whitney Museum of American Art.
