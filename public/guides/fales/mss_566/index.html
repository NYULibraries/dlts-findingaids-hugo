Abstract

The East 13th Street Band was founded in 1980 by David C. Levy and Larry Rivers. The band's name was derived from Larry Rivers' street address in New York, where the band often practiced. Other band members included Myron Blackman, Howard Brofsky, Jeff Brown, Howard Kanovitz, Leslie Silver, and Charlie Toor. The band actively recorded and performed throughout the United States during the 1980's and early 1990's. This collection contains audio recordings, along with their session track sheets for recording sessions from 1981. 1984-1985.

Scope and Contents

This collection contains audio recordings with session track sheets for East 13th Street Band recording sessions in New York, at both Nemperor Records and NOLA Recording Studios, 1981, 1984-1985.
