Abstract

This collection was assembled by gallery owner Theodore Bonin, containing 61 artworks and publications from over 20 artists including Tom Otterness, Lisa Bowman, Judy Rifka, Janine Antoni, and Luis Frangella, who were active in New York's downtown art scene during the 1980s and 1990s. Artwork mediums include hand colored and painted works on paper, wood sculpture, ceramic sculptures, prints, and assemblages.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Donated by Theodore Bonin in December, 2016; the accession number associated with this gift is 2017.031.

Biographical Note

Theodore Bonin is an art collector and part owner of Alexander and Bonin, a New York art gallery established in 1995, whose primary purpose is to represent and support the work of emerging, mid-career, and established international contemporary artists.

Scope and Contents

This collection contains 61 artworks and publications, accumulated by Theodore Bonin over many years, from over 20 artists including Tom Otterness, Lisa Bowman, Judy Rifka, Janine Antoni, and Luis Frangella, who were active in New York's downtown art scene during the 1980s and 1990s. Artwork media represented in this collection include painted or drawn works on paper, wood sculpture, ceramic sculptures, prints, and assemblages, all produced during the 1980s and 1990s. This collection illustrates popular mediums and subject matter in artworks in this community of artists in New York.
