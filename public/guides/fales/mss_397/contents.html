Series I: Artwork, 2004-2010

Scope and Content

This series contains artwork created by Dzubilo using various mediums on paper and cloth. Majority of the work relates to life or advocacy for people with HIV/AIDS and transgender people. Some art also is either created with or about Dzubilo's partner T De Long.

Arrangement

Series I is arranged alphabetically by title of the artwork. If there is no title, the folders are arranged chronologically.

Series II: Photographs, 1983-2004

Scope and Content

This series contains both professional and personal photographs of Dzubilo, her family, and friends. Alice O'Malley and Katrina del Mar frequently photographed Dzubilo, and their work can be found throughout the series. Dzubilo's noted can also be found on the back of most photographs.

Arrangement

Series II is arranged in original order the photographs were found in the collection.
