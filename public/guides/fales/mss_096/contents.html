Series I: Manuscripts, Undated

Scope and Contents note

Series I contains the manuscript files of Sparrow's poetry and fiction, most of which are in the form of typescripts (some with corrections) or handwritten notes. A large number of these files are manuscript fragments pertaining to an unidentified novel and are labeled as such. The author's original grouping of manuscript fragments is maintained.
