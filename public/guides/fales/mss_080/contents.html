Series I: Appearances

Scope and Contents note

The series consists primarily of notebooks compiled by Witz over his years as publisher of the magazine. Included within the notebooks are poems, drawings, phone numbers, and other assorted items. Also included in the series are financial documents and artwork from the magazine, as well as notebooks from before the beginning of the magazine.

Series IV: 1997 Accretion, early 1960s-2008

Scope and Contents

This accretion contains Bob Witz's sketchbooks (1974-1997), Stencil Novel (1978-1980), chapbooks (undated), submissions to Appearances Magazine, photographs, correspondence (1983-1997), newspapers (1970-1972), catalogues and ephemera from galleries (1983-1997). The accretion includes notes and writings by Bobby Joe Burbridge (late 1960s - early 1970s), and writing by Julie Gross.

Sketchbooks, Chapbooks, Photographs, Correspondence, Newspapers; Notes and Writings by Bobby Joe Burbridge, D.A. Levy and Julie Gross; Gallery Catalogues and Ephemera, Early 1960s-2008

Series VIII: Undated Accretion(s), 1977-2010

Scope and Contents

This accretion contains professional and personal correspondence (1977-2008), gallery catalogues and ephemera (1977-2008), submissions of writing and photographs to Appearances Magazine (undated, 1982, 1999, 2002-2010) and some Appearances production files, Appearances Press publications (1981-1986), a typescript of Don't Wait; Or, Wait, a novel by Robert Witz (undated), Bob Witz's sketchbooks (undated, 1990-2013) and some business records (circa 1980-1986) including Appearances certificate of incorporation (1983) and a list of subscribers (undated). There are also seven small flat round objects (1980s).
