Series I: Librettos and Playbills

Scope and Content

This series is comprised of original and photocopied librettos for Broadway and Off-Broadway productions. The bulk of this series consists of the librettos, which are in various stages of production, ranging from final versions, rehearsal, and draft copies. Many of the librettos include annotations and edits, which are noted in the finding aid. Also included in this series are playbills and newspaper clippings of theater reviews.
