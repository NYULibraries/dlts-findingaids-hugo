Scope and Contents

This accretion to the William F. Wu Comics Collection contains comic books, pulp magazines, and books dating from 1920 to 2001. These publications consist of racist and xenophobic Asian stereotypes and include the comic Master of Kung Fu and reproductions of 1930s pulp magazines.

Arrangement

This series has not been arranged by an archivist. The materials are arranged in the order in which they were received from the donor.
