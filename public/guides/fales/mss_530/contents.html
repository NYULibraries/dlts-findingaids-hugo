Scope and Contents

Series VIII. Weinperson, 1993-2001

Scope and Contents

The Weinperson series consists of a numerical series of photocopied postcards and production material for the art piece ANNOUNCEMENT. This material dates from 1993 to 2001 and was created by Braun using her Weinperson persona exploring her visual interpretations of racial and sexual innuendo. The finished product of 152 post cards are arranged together at the beginning of the series and then the preparation for each week's art piece is collected in numbered folders.

Series X. Posters, 1990-1995

Scope and Contents

The Posters series contains gridwork that was integral to Braun's colossal wall art photocopying process. This art process material is from her exhibitions in between 1990 and 1995. This collection contains gridwork posters from her exhibitions at Sonyun Yi Gallery in New York from 1990; the University of Alabama in Tuscaloosa, Alabama from 1991; On The Wall Gallery in New York from 1990; Columbia University in New York from 1991; the Drawing Center in New York from 1990; the Künstlerhaus Bethanien in Berlin, Germany from 1992; and the New Museum for Contemporary Art in New York in 1994.
