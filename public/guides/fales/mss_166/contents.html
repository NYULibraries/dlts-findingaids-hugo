Series I: Correspondence

Scope and Contents note

This series contains Godzilla's correspondence from 1991-2001, as well as letters to send to artists and files kept by Lynne Yamamoto and Godzilla founder Ken Chu. Ken Chu's folders contain notices of upcoming art exhibitions sent to him by artitsts, two slides of his own work, and a newspaper clipping. Lynne Yamamoto's folder, separated into two parts, holds correspondence and administrative documents she collected.

Series V: Related Organizations

Scope and Contents note

Series V comprises various documents, articles, and publications either published by or related to organizations related to Godzilla. Some organizations, such as the Asian American Arts Alliance, collaborated with Godzilla; others simply had a similar purpose. Series V contains three subseries: Documents, Publications, and Items of Interest to Godzilla. Series A: Documents houses ideas for collaboratory projects, financial information, and operating information about related organizations. Subseries B: Publications contains journals, newsletters, and exhibition brochures from similar arts organizations. Subseries C: Items of Interest to Godzilla holds newspaper clippings and articles collected by Godzilla that either feature Godzilla and/or a related organization, or address a cause related to Godzilla's purpose.
