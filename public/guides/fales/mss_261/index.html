Abstract

Miscellany Magazine ran from 1929 - 1931 and comprised 6 issues. The magazine featured poetry, essays, and film, art, and literature reviews. It was based in New York City. The collection contains correspondence, advertisers, contributions, subscriber lists, and reviews of Miscellany Magazine. There is also one issue of the magazine.

Biographical / Historical

Miscellany Magazine ran from 1929 - 1931 and comprised 6 issues. It was edited by Frederick W. Dupee, Geoffrey T. Hellman, George L. Kingsland Morris, and Dwight MacDonald. The magazine featured poetry, essays, and film, art, and literature reviews. It was based in New York City.
