Series III: Einstein

Scope and Contents note

Contains materials related to the production of Zackheim's second novel, Einstein's Daughter: The Search for Lieserl, a biography of Einstein's illegitimate daughter, conceived out of wedlock when Einstein and Mileva Maric were students at Zurich Polytechnic Institute. These files were collected over the five year period in which Zackheim investigated the possible fates of Lieserl. Organized into nine subseries.

Subseries A: Research

Scope and Contents note

Contains Michele Zackheim's research files that include notes, correspondence, books such as biographies and tourist guides, and photographs for Einstein's Daughter. The subseries is arranged alphabetically by author when relevant and subject with the exception of Michele Zackheim's note card file and research binders, which can be found at the end of the subseries. Research Binder # 4 consisting of negatives, contact sheets, and photographs was removed and transferred to subseries G, box 11.
