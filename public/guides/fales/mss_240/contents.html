Series I: Trinidad Theater Workshop Subject Files

Dates:
1958-2007

langmaterial

English

Scope and Contents

Series comprises play programs, play scripts, and clippings related almost exclusively the Trinidad Theater Workshop, very little relating to the Little Carib Theater, and some clippings about Derek Walcott plays performed elsewhere than at the Trinidad Theater Workshop and in Trinidad.

Arrangement

This series is arranged into five subseries:
Subseries A: Productions Written by Derek Walcott
Subseries B: Productions Written by Others
Subseries C: Laurence Goldstraw Subject Files
Subseries D: General Trinidad Theater Workshop Subject Files
Subseries E: The Little Carib Theater

Series II: Derek Walcott Subject Files

Dates:
1945-2008

langmaterial

English

Scope and Contents

Series comprises periodicals containing poems, essays, and reviews by Derek Walcott, some small publications of Walcott's, photocopies of poems by Walcott, and reviews of and essays on Walcott's work as well as general articles on Walcott.

Arrangement

This series is arranged into two subseries:
Subseries A: Work by Derek Walcott
Subseries B: Work about Derek Walcott (Press, Reviews)
