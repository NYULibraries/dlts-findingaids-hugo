Series I: Red Hot + Blue

Scope and Contents

Red Hot + Blue was released in 1990 and was the first in the Red Hot series. Red Hot + Blue featured contemporary pop performers reinterpreting the songs of Cole Porter. An accompanying television special featured videos for all but two songs.

In 2006 Red Hot + Blue was re-issued on a special two-disc set including the original CD remastered, and a DVD of the home video.

(Reference: http://www.redhot.org)

Series II: Red Hot + Dance

Dates:
1992

Scope and Contents

Red Hot + Dance was released in 1992 by Columbia Records, featuring three exclusive tracks from George Michael. Other major dance artists donated tracks, remixed by D.J.s and producers from around the world. An accompanying home video was released by Sony Music Video Enterprises. (Reference: http://www.redhot.org)

An anomaly exists in Series II: Red Hot + Dance. An additional series, designated as Subseries L, pertains to the legal dispute between George Michael and Sony Music.
