Scope and Contents

This accretion includes recipe booklets and calendars, a French cruise liner menu from 1953, and information booklet on the food of Denmark from 1962. The recipe booklets were primarily published as promotional material by companies including Oklahoma Peanut and Pecan Commissions, Oklahoma Natural Gas Company, Giroux Grenadine Syrup, and California Raisin Advisory Board. The recipe books typically contain special recipes for products such has raisins or pecans, alcohol, or for specific holidays like Christmas. This accretion also has recipe books produced by Jim Loyd's Open Line and Family Favorites, which appears to have been a radio show. The calendars are also promotional items with recipes, sponsored by the Farmers' Almanac, Argo and Kingsford, and Cuisinart, dating from the late 1970s to the 1990s.
