Scope and Contents

This series contains documentation, photography, invoices, and correspondence pertaining to bands whose work was distributed by or related to the Outpunk label. Bands include Tribe 8, Team Dresch, Pansy Division, God is My Co-Pilot, and Cyryus.

Series III: Phonograph Records,

Scope and Contents

This series contains albums released by the Outpunk label. Bands include Tribe 8, Cypher in the Snow, God is My Co-Pilot, Hyperdrive Kittens, Pansy Division, and Sta-Prest.

Conditions Governing Access

Audio materials are not accessible for listening.
