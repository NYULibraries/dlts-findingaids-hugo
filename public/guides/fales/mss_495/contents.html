Series I. Print and Media Publications

Scope and Contents

The print and media publications' series consists of mostly earmarked magazines and newspaper articles that were a part of Michael Wilke and the Commercial Closet Association's research ranging from 1975-2017. Publications included within this series include Pride, Passport, Out, Outtraveler, Attitude, The Advocate, Genre, and The New York Times.

Series II. Photographs, 1986-2003

31142063130705box: 21 (Material Type: Mixed Materials)

Scope and Contents

The photograph series is comprised of Michael Wilke's personal photographs of family reunions, vacations and LGBT community events. Most of the photographs in this series range between 1986-2003. There are four folders with undated photographs, which are most likely from the early to mid 1900s.
