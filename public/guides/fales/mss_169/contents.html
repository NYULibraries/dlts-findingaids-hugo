Series I: Subject Files, 1962-1995; undated

langmaterial

English

Scope and Content

This series comprises folders created by Lippard on subjects relating to non-Western/cross-cultural art and artists. The series contains some correspondence (mostly postcard announcements), which were left in place to preserve the original order. Some unlabeled folders that appeared to be subject files are labeled "[Unidentified subject file]" and placed near the end of the series.

Material in this series is arranged alphabetically by folder name.

langmaterial

English

Scope and Content

This series comprises all correspondence in the collection not contained in the Subject Files series. Not all correspondence is to/from Lippard.

Material in this series is arranged chronologically by earliest date on folder. Some folders contain wide ranges of dates, but material were kept together to preserve original order.

Series IV: Printed Material, 1968-1996; undated

langmaterial

English

Scope and Content

Series comprises miscellaneous printed material found either foldered in unlabeled folders or loose in boxes. Materials are almost exclusively gallery announcements, invitations, press releases, and exhibition catalogs. Series contains some correspondence, mostly postcard announcements, left in place to preserve original order. Some items in this series are stamped "RECEIVED; [date]; LEO LERMAN."

Material in this series is arranged chronologically by earliest date on folder.
