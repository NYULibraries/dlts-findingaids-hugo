Series I: Unpublished Manuscripts, Originals and Carbon Copies,

Scope and Contents note

Series 1 contains 4 boxes containing original and carbon copy typewritten manuscripts of Pickett's unpublished fiction. Pickett wrote under his own name and various pseudonyms. The manuscripts are grouped by pseudonym and arranged alphabetically by title. Series 2 contains a sherry glass box fashioned by Ralph E. Pickett.
