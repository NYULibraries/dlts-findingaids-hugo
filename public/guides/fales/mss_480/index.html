Abstract

The Julie Kathryn Franco I am Snow Angel Papers is composed of the works of music artist Julie Kathryn Franco between 2014-2016 under her performance alias I Am Snow Angel, which she uses to produce electronica music. The collection includes sound recordings from 5 EPs, 1 full-length album, 6 singles and 5 music videos, all written and performed by I Am Snow Angel. It also includes the website iamsnowangel.com, which includes a brief biography of the artist, performance videos, audio recordings, and articles featuring Julie Kathryn Franco and her work as I Am Snow Angel.

Biographical Note

Julie Kathryn Franco is a female music artist who grew up in Lake Placid, New York and currently lives in New York City. She was a social worker for several years before she began recording her music. She was raised performing music, and has experimented with several styles in her recording career. She put out her first Americana Noir EP, "Broken Love", in 2012 under the name Julie Kathryn, followed by her first full-length album, "Black Trees", in 2013. In 2014, she embarked on a new musical project under the name I Am Snow Angel to explore self-produced electronica.

Scope and Contents

The Julie Kathryn Franco I Am Snow Angel Papers documents the work of female music artist Julie Kathryn Franco under her performance alias I Am Snow Angel between 2014-2016. The collection contains 41 sound recordings and 5 video recordings made by Franco; an archived copy of iamsnowangel.com, the website owned and operated by Franco; 18 image files of album art; and one spreadsheet created by Franco with information about each recording. The website includes a brief biography of the artist, performance videos, audio recordings, and articles featuring Franco and her work as I Am Snow Angel. The image files are digital copies of the artwork included with her albums, the creator(s) of which are unknown. These files are arranged with the sound recordings for the relevant albums. The spreadsheet includes information about the recordings, including format, title, and release information.
