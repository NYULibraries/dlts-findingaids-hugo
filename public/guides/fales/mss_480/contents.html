box: Electronic records (Material Type: electronic records)

Extent

Scope and Contents

The I Am Snow Angel website contains information about Julie Kathryn's work as a recording artist, including videos, audio, and images of her work and performances. It also includes tour dates and news about music releases and press information. The main website embeds properties from her Soundcloud, Youtube, and Instagram pages, which are also a part of the capture of her web presence.

Accruals

New site crawls are accrued quarterly.

Appraisal

Crawl was limited to domains and subdomains of iamsnowangel.com in order to remain within the collection scope and data constraints.

Robots.txt (a piece of code designed to limit crawler activity within a website) was ignored on Instagram, Soundcloud, and Youtube profiles.

Embedded Soundcloud audio were captured, but do not render in the archived copy. Future changes to the Wayback Machine may allow the files to be played.

Physical Characteristics and Technical Requirements

Due to technical or privacy issues, archived websites may not be exact copies of the original website at the time of the web crawl. Certain file types will not be captured dependent on how they are embedded in the site. Other parts of websites that the crawler has difficulty capturing includes Javascript, streaming content, database-driven content, and highly interactive content. Full-Text searches of archived websites are available at https://archive-it.org/organizations/567.
