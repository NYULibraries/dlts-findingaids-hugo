Scope and Content

The Riot Grrrl Publicity Collection consists of magazine and newspaper articles published about Bikini Kill and the Riot Grrrl Movement in the early to mid 1990s.

Conditions Governing Access

Materials are open to researchers. Please contact the Fales Library and Special Collections, fales.library@nyu.edu, 212-998-2596.

Copyright (or related rights to publicity and privacy) for materials in this collection was not transferred to New York University. Permission to use materials must be secured from the copyright holder. Please contact the Fales Library and Special Collections, fales.library@nyu.edu, 212-998-2596.

Arrangement

The Collection is arranged chronologically.

Provenance

The items were collected by Bikini Kill bassist Kathi Wilcox's mother during the early to mid 1990s. Wilcox gave the collection to bandmate Kathleen Hanna, who donated it to NYU in 2011.
