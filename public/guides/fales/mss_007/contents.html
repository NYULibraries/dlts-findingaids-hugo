Series I: Correspondence and Issues of Burning Water

Scope and Contents note

Series 1 contains one box of correspondence to the editors, Edward Kissam and Paul Boorstin between 1962-1968. Noted correspondents include Carol Bergé, Robert Bly, Ted Enslin, Clayton Eschleman, and Gary Snyder. Series 1 also contains issues 1-5 of Burning Water.
