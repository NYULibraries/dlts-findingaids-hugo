Scope and Content Note

The Burning Water Archive is a collection of correspondence to the editors, Edward Kissam and Paul Boorstin, from 1962 to 1968. Collection highlights include letters written by Carol Bergé, Robert Bly, Ted Enslin, Clayton Eschleman, and Gary Snyder.
