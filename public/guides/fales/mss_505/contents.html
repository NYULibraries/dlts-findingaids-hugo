Series I: Journal of research citations and photocopies of research files, Early 1800s - late 1890s

Scope and Contents

This series contains Kenneth Silverman's journal of research citations and photocopies of his research files for his 1991 book Edgar Allen Poe: Mournful and Never-Ending Remembrance. The research files are categorized by person, place and time period and materials, although mostly photocopied, are extracted from primary and secondary sources. The photocopied research materials give an overview of Edgar Allan Poe's personal and professional life ranging from his childhood, family history and romantic relationships with alleged fiance Sarah Elmira Royster and wife Virginia Poe. They consist of correspondence and journal entries from various manuscript and archival collections across the United States, maps of Poe's homes in various cities such as Baltimore and Philadelphia, newspaper articles and book extractions from about 1800 to the late 1890s.

Arrangement

This series has not been arranged by an archivist. The materials are arranged in the order in which they were received from the donor.

Series II: Index cards of research notes and annotations , Circa 1980s

Scope and Contents

This series contains Kenneth Silverman's index cards of research notes for his 1991 book Edgar Allen Poe: Mournful and Never-Ending Remembrance. These notes are categorized, handwritten and typed in full sentence/paragraph form, including annotations and supporting quotes from the documents in his research files. Notecard categorization include notes Poe in Philadelphia and New York in 1837 as well as notes on Poe's personality traits as stated by those who knew him.

Arrangement

This series has not been arranged by an archivist. The materials are arranged in the order in which they were received from the donor.
