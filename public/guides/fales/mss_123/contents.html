Series II. Performance Notes and Ephemera

Dates:
1973-2001

Extent

.5 linear feet in 1 manuscript box

Scope and Contents

This series consists of ephemera, programs, and publications concerning dance performances in New York City in the 1970s and 1980s.

Arrangement

This series has not been arranged by an archivist. The materials are arranged in the order in which they were received from the donor.
