Historical note

"Only Human: HIV-Negative Gay Men in the AIDS Epidemic" is a documentary that explores the relationship between HIV risk reduction practices and the advent of AIDS. The documentary includes interviews that draw from the sexual experiences of HIV-negative men from a variety of backgrounds. "Only Human" simultaneously follows the dramatic struggle of one gay Latino youth's to balance health with sexuality.

Scope and Content

Only Human: HIV-negative Gay Men in the AIDS Epidemic Archive is comprised of source material, work-in-progress sample reels, masters, and the final cut of the film "Only Human: HIV-negative Gay Men in the AIDS Epidemic." The film was directed by Ioannis Mookas, written by David Deitcher, and co-produced by Mookas, Deitcher, and Tina DiFeliciantonio. The videorecordings span from 1995 to 1998.

The collection is arranged in one series:

Series I: Videorecordings: contains 2 boxes of VHS and Betacam SP.
