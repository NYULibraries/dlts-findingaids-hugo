Series III: Prose, Poetry, and Other Writing

Scope and Contents note

This series includes prose, poetry, reviews, and other creative writing by Stuart Sherman from his teenage years up until his death.

Arrangement

The material has been ordered chronologically in groups respective of the arrangement as received: Juvenilia; Notes and Poetry; Reviews and Prose; Poetry; and Diagram Poetry. Although attempts were made to isolate these groups within this series, researchers may find Sherman's poetry and prose mixed throughout the collection. For example, poetry may be found in address books, in notebooks titled prose writing, alongside performance planning, or in other places.
