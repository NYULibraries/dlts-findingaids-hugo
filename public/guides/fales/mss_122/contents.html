Series IV: Photographic Material

Extent

5.65 Linear Feet 27 photo binders

Scope and Content note

The photographic material of Series IV spans the entirety of Foreman's career, from early dance performances through later art installations in the nineteen-nineties. Performances from the Laura Foreman Dance Company are very well documented, as are the numerous derivations of Foreman's "Birdhouse as Metaphor" series. Photographers of Foreman's dance performances include Robert Alexander, Peter Moore and Milton Oleaga.

Arrangement note

The photographic material is arranged thematically and chronologically, similarly to the list of works in the Biographical Note above. Stage works are documented first, followed by artworks, followed by specific installations and exhibitions, and concluding with personal and unidentified photos. Each box is annotated with the photographic processes contained within.
