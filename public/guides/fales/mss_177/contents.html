Scope and Contents note

The Journals/Notebooks Series consists of spiral notebooks, bound notebooks, loose pages, and legal pads containing diary entries, drafts of poetry and correspondence, and business notes, arranged chronologically. The date reflects the date the journal begins.

Series II: Correspondence and Subject Series

Scope and Contents note

This series consists of interfiled correspondence folders and subject folders, arranged alphabetically by correspondent or subject, and chronologically within the folders. Ed Smith's original arrangement has been kept, and thus alphebetization is occasionally by first name, more often by last name, and by subject. Where no folders existed for loose materials, new subject or correspondence files were created and interfiled with Smith's. Folders that were not part of Smith's arrangement scheme are indicated by brackets around the title. The folders contain correspondence, flyers, photographs, and ephemera.
