Abstract

Marina Urbach is New York City-based an artist and curator. This collection consists of materials collected by Urbach related to C Space gallery in New York City in the late 1970s. Materials include exhibition announcement cards and copies of a catalog for the 1976 "Style and Process" exhibition organized by Urbach. Of note is a exhibition announcement for the 1977 "Memory" exhibition at C Space organized by Marcia Tucker in cooperation with the New Museum.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Biographical Note

Marina Urbach is New York City-based an artist and curator who oversaw the C Space gallery. In 1976 she organized the exhibition "Style and Process" at the Fine Arts Building.

Scope and Contents

This collection consists of materials collected by curator and artist Marina Urbach related to C Space Gallery in New York City. Materials include exhibition announcement cards and copies of a catalog for the 1976 "Style and Process" exhibition organized by Urbach. Of note is a exhibition announcement for the 1977 "Memory" exhibition at C Space organized by Marcia Tucker in cooperation with the New Museum.
