Series II: Zines, Notebooks, and Papers

Scope and Content

Series II: Zines, Notebooks, and Papers contains original zines, Molly's personal notebooks/diaries, press clippings pertaining to her musical career, and uncategorized papers collected in the course of her studies and travels.

Girl Germs No. 3, undated

31142054894277box: 3, folder: 3 (Material Type: Mixed Materials)
Abstract

Olympia-based Bratmobile members Molly Neuman and Allison Wolfe begin this issue of their riot grrrl zine with reminiscence on female solidarity and letters from readers. Contributions include an extended piece on the Asian experience in the United States and resulting struggles to maintain ethnic identity in the face of discrimination. Other themes include lesbian relations, violence against women, and sexual abuse. Further elements include a column debunking masturbation myths, serialized fiction, an extended top ten list, reviews of video artist Sadie Benning and new music, an interview with Rebecca of Love Child, and a list of zines. Concludes with a narrative describing the origins of Girl Germs.
