Abstract

Fisher & Levy was a New York City catering business established by Chip Fisher and Doug Levy in 1983. Fisher & Levy offered high-end, corporate catering services as well as individual takeout items. This collection contains materials created and collected by the Fisher & Levy catering company. Materials include press clippings, correspondence from customers, certificates, and menus. Materials date from 1978 to 2006.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Donated by Chip Fisher, 2013. The accession number associated with this gift is 2013.372.

Historical Note

Fisher & Levy was a New York City catering business established by Chip Fisher and Doug Levy in 1983. Fisher & Levy offered daily corporate catering services as well as individual takeout items from their storefront location in Sutton Place. The catering company focused on providing high-end meals and food items tailored to appeal to a sophisticated, corporate clientele. Chip Fisher, a former IBM salesperson, handled the overall business, while Doug Levy, chef, was in charge of the cooking.

Scope and Contents

This collection contains materials created and collected by the Fisher & Levy catering company. A majority of the collection contains press clippings regarding the company, and the owners Chip Fisher and Doug Levy. A smaller portion of the collection contains correspondence from customers, certificates, and menus. Materials date from 1978 to 2006.
