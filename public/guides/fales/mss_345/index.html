Abstract

The Larry Miller Fluxus Collection contains photographs and ephemera related to Flux-Tours conducted by Larry Miller.

Provenance

The Larry Miller Fluxus Collection was donated to NYU's Fales Library and Special Collections by Larry Miller and Sara Seagull, 2012.

Biographical Note

Larry Miller has been associated with the Fluxus school since the 1970s. One of his significant contributions to the scene was the "Flux-Tour", a performance model whereby artists and performers led alternative gallery and museum tours. Rather than discuss or interpret any artworks on display, the guides turned their attention to the spaces themselves, examining in minute detail such subjects as the lighting, floors, and other structural elements to which their attention was drawn.

In 2011, Miller performed a Flux-Tour at the Grey Gallery, in conjunction with its exhibitions, Fluxus and the Essential Questions of Life and Fluxus at NYU: Before and Beyond. The exhibitions featured documentation from prior Flux-Tours, dating back to 1976.

Scope and Content

The Larry Miller Fluxus Collection contains photographs and ephemera related to Miller's Flux-Tours, as documented in the Grey Gallery exhibitions, Fluxus and the Essential Questions of Life and Fluxus at NYU: Before and Beyond.
