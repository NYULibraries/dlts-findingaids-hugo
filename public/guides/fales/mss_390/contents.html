Scope and Content

This series contains the fliers announcing concerts for each season of the Washington Square Contemporary Music Society and programs for most of the concerts, with progams notes. It also contains press clippings and review of concerts.

Series III: Audio

Arrangement

Media is arranged by format, and chronologically within each format. The formats are 1/4" Reel to Reel audio, 1/4" Audiocassette, DAT, and CD.

Conditions Governing Access

Audiovisual materials have not been preserved and may not be available to researchers.
