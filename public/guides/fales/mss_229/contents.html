Series I: Roundtable Meetings and Events, 1983-2001

Arrangement Note

Materials are arranged chronologically, as received from RWF. Each folder listed contains a number of folders labeled by RWF administrators.

Scope and Content

This series contains materials related to public and member events orchestrated by the Roundtable for Women in Foodservice. Regular meetings and panel discussions are addressed in this series, as well as special events and award ceremonies. Yearly calendars are interspersed throughout the series.
