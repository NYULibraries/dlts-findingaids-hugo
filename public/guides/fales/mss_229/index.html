Abstract

The Roundtable for Women in Foodservice, Inc., is an organization devoted to the advancement of women in all segments of the foodservice industry. This collection contains materials related to the Roundtable's operations between 1983 and 2001.

Provenance

This collection was donated to the Fales Library by Roundtable for Women in Foodservice, Inc. in 2008.

Biographical / Historical

The Roundtable for Women in Foodservice, Inc. (RWF) was founded in 1983 in New York City. From its inception, its mission has been to aid the career advancement of women within all creative, administrative, and entrepreneurial segments of the foodservice industry.

Scope and Content

The materials in the collection relate to the administration and operation of the Roundtable for Women in Foodservice between 1983 and 2001. The collection contains documentation of the Roundtable's public events and board meetings, as well as publications by and about the Roundtable.

Arrangement

Materials in the collection are arranged entirely as they were received, with the exception of all multimedia items, which were removed to Series IV. All efforts were made to preserve the original arrangement of the materials; the series in the collection and their titles reflect RWF's own arrangement strategy.
