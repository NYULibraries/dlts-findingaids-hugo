Series II: Prints by theme

Scope and Content note

Themes have been identified by labels existing within the estate or by visual similarity. Annotations or labels that were written by Jimmy De Sana are indicated in the item description by his initials (JDS).

Items from the Portrait theme are organized alphabetically by the subject's last name. Where a last name is absent slides are organized alphabetically by first name. These are followed by unidentified subjects.
