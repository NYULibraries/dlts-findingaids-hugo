Series I: Correspondence,

Scope and Contents note

Series 1 contains one box of correspondence to John Taggert, editor of Maps. Noted correspondents include Carol Bergé, Paul Blackburn, Hayden Carruth, Robert Creeley, Guy Davenport, Gary Snyder, and Louis Zukofsky.
