Series I: Correspondence and Manuscripts,

Scope and Contents note

Series 1 contains one box of correspondence and manuscripts to Tom Raworth, the editor of Outburst [and the Matrix Press]. Contributing writers include Carol Bergé, Paul Blackburn, William Burroughs, Robert Creeley, Lawrence Ferlinghetti, Allen Ginsberg, Anselm Hollo, LeRoi Jones, Denise Levertov, Michael McClure, Henry Miller, Alain Robbe-Grillet, and Gary Snyder.
