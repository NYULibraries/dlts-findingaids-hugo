Series I: Appetite for Life: The Biography of Julia Child

Scope and Content

This series consists of correspondence, newspaper and magazine clippings, interview transcripts, photographs, photocopies of original letters and diaries, legal documents, manuscript drafts, proofs, notes and publicity materials. It also contains post-publication materials including correspondence and reviews responding to the book, correspondence relating to film rights, and materials related to articles and writing by Noël Riley Fitch about Julia Child after completion of the biography.

Subseries A: Research Files

Scope and Content

Subseries A: Research Files is arranged primarily following the structure of Appetite for Life, which loosely corresponds to the chronology of Julia Child's life. Research materials about Julia Child subsequent to the publication of Appetite for Life are arranged chonologically. The subseries consists of magazines and newspaper clippings related to Julia Child's life, work, and influences; photocopies of original diaries and letters of Paul and Julia Child; and notes and segments of transcripts from interviews conducted by Noël Riley Fitch about Julia Child.
