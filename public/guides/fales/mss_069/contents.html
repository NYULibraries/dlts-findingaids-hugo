Scope and Contents

The bulk of the collection, this series includes typed and handwritten poems, and photocopied manuscripts of Swenson's published works. Most of the poems are undated and include Swenson's annotations. This series includes four audiocassettes. Cassette labels indicate three audio recordings of poetry readings and one sample poetry reading.
