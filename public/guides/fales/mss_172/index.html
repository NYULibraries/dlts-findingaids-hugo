Abstract

This collection is comprised of materials documenting Jerry G. Dickason's and Elly Dickason's affiliation with the Judson Memorial Church and its associated organizations, including materials related to the production of the book Remembering Judson House, which was published in 2000. These materials date between 1920 and 2012 and include correspondence, manuscripts, research notes, research materials, galley proofs, board minutes, photographs, newspaper clippings, transcripts of sermons, church bulletins, artwork, and media.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

The Dickason Papers were initially donated to NYU in 2002. Accretions followed in 2007 and 2013. A third accretion was donated by Jerry G. Dickason in April 2016; the accession number associated with this gift is 2016.051.

Biographical / Historical

Jerry G. Dickason, a retired professor, and Elly Dickason, a long-time member of the Judson Church congregation, edited the book Remembering Judson House (Judson Memorial Church, 2000), a compilation of 48 interviews with people who lived or worked at Judson House.

Processing Information

Decisions about the arrangement, description, and physical interventions taken on this collection prior to 2016 have not yet been recorded. In December 2016, an accretion was incorporated into the collection as a separate series. Some materials were treated for mold and then rehoused in acid-free folders and an archival manuscript box. Materials were kept in the order in which they were received from the donor, and interleaving and Mylar sleeves were used for acidic newsprint and loose photographs.

On February 2019, materials from accession 2008.172 were placed in new acid-free folders and manuscript boxes, rehoused for offsite storage. Original order of documents maintained from boxes 20-23.

Arrangement

This collection is arranged into five series: Series I: Production Materials; Series II: Source Materials and Research; Series III: Administrative Materials Unrelated to "Remembering Judson House;" Series IV: Hot Spots Calendar (includes Production Materials), and Series V: 2016 Accretion.

Physical Characteristics and Technical Requirements

Audiovisual and born-digital materials have not been preserved and may not be available to researchers.
