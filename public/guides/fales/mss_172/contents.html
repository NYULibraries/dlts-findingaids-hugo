2016 Accretion

Scope and Contents

The accretion consists of materials that date between 1969 and 2012 with the bulk of the materials dating between the early 1970s and the early 1990s. Within the accretion are transcripts of sermons (including one by Elly Dickason), Judson Memorial Church bulletins, photographs used in Remembering Judson House, as well as memorial materials about the passings of composer Al Carmines and Minister Howard R. Moody. Also included in the accretion are materials about Dickason's and former Judson employee Jim Covington's involvement with the New York Center for Men (1983-1984), as well as a file about the research of anti-war activist Maarten Knottenbelt (1982-1989).

Arrangement

This accretion has not been arranged by an archivist; materials are arranged in the order in which they were received by the donor.
