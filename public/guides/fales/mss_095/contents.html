Series I: Literary properties,

Scope and Content

Series I: Literary properties consists of correspondence, memos, contracts, agreements, drafts, royalty statements and notes related to the financial and legal aspects of novels, plays, short stories, articles and screenplays by Erich Maria Remarque, as well as related adaptations. It is organized into subseries arranged alphabetically by work title. Within each subseries, materials are arranged chronologically, and photocopies are filed based on dates of the originals.
