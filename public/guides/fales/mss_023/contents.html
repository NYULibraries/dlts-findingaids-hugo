Series I: Correspondence to Peter Finch, editor

Scope and Contents note

Series I contains six boxes of correspondence to Peter Finch concerning the magazine Second Aeon. Noted correspondents include Paul Auster, Carol Berg, Robert Bly, Charles Bukowski, William Burroughs, John Cage, Cyril Hodges, Alison Knowles, Jim Morrison, Jeff Nuttall, and Peter Redgrove.

Series II: Editorial Correspondence, 1967-1974

Scope and Contents note

Series II contains four boxes of correspondence to Peter Finch pertaining to editorial concerns of the magazines uch as: subscription requests, poetry admissions, thank you/congratulatory notes, and other such solicitations. This series also includes Peter Finch's personal correspondence comprising a copybook and letters dealing with his career as a poet.
