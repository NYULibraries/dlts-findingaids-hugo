Sheila Lukins Papers

Contents

Series I: Professional Files

Dates:
1976-2007

Scope and Content

Series comprises material relating to Sheila Lukins role as food writer, cookbook author, and proprietor of the Silver Palate. Materials include correspondence; recipes; drafts for food columns at Parade; correspondence, publicity, and other materials related to her cookbooks; press and clippings for Lukins, her cookbooks, or the Silver Palate.

Arrangement

Subseries A, C, D, E, and G are arranged chronologically. Undated material is filed at the end of the series in alphabetical order by folder label.
Subseries B and F are arranged alphabetically by folder label.

Subseries G: Miscellaneous Professional Files

Dates:
1991-2007

Scope and Content

Subseries comprises material that could not be integrated into the main subseries but still reflects some aspect of Lukins's professional life, including material related to Lukins's travel and research on international cuisine, her involvement with Les Dames d'Escoffier, her student records and work on art education, an NYU Distinguished Alumni Achievement Award she received, and her early catering work.

That said, there is some material in this subseries that could easily fit into one or more of the other, more specific subseries above; however, the archivist thought it best to leave this material, in the order in which he received them.
