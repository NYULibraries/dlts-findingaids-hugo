Series I: Photographic Material,

Scope and Content

This series is made up of photographs sent to Ladyfest organizer Tina Heschelman from organizers and participants in the event, after it was over. The materials include photographs, negatives, contact sheets, slides and the accompanying envelopes and correspondence found with the photographic material.

Series II: Beth Stinson Material,

Scope and Content

This series is made up of material collected and/or created by Beth Stinson in her role as an organizer of Ladyfest 2000. The materials include paste-ups, mock-ups, and ephemera.

Scope and Content
