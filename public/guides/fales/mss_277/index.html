Abstract

A's events was a series, curated by Arleen Schloss, that was part traditional art show, part salon, and part performance, occuring at 330 Broome Street. The A's Events Flyers Collection includes flyers for A's events from 1979-1987, as well as some flyers for events at other spaces.

Biographical / Historical

A's Events began in 1979 as an art show and performance occuring weekly on Wednesday nights at a loft space at 330 Broome Street in Manhattan. The events combined a 2D art show with performances, music, readings, and other forms of artistic production. Arleen Schloss, a pioneer in NYC performance art, curated the series.

Scope and Content

The flyers contained in this collection document A's Events from 1979-1987. A's events was a series, curated by Arleen Schloss, that was part traditional art show, part salon, and part performance, occuring at 330 Broome Street. The collection also contains flyers for other events, including musical performances, readings, and holiday parties at a variety of other venues in Downtown Manhattan. A number of notable downtown artists are mentioned on the flyers, including: Eric Bogosian, David Wojnarowicz, Carolee Schneeman, Claes Oldenberg, and others.

Arrangement

The flyers are arranged chronologically by year. Within folders that denote a particular year, flyers are arranged by specific date. Undated flyers are at the end of the series.

Provenance
