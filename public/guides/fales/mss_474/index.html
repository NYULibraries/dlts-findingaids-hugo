Abstract

Jarrod Anderson is an artist/illustrator. He graduated from Ringling School of Art and Design in 2003. Anderson was the illustrator for The Weaklings (2008), written by Dennis Cooper. He has also exhibited his artwork in various galleries. Anderson's collection consists of nine very fine line graphite drawings, with a similar look to etching. Works were executed in 2006, 2007, and some undated. Subjects consist of human figures, everyday objects, and vignettes.

Scope and Contents

This collection consists of nine drawings by artist/illustrator Jarrod Anderson, executed between 2006-2007. The works are very fine line drawings in graphite, with a similar look to etching. Subjects consist of human figures, every day objects, and vignettes.
