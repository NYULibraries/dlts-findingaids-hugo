Series I: Production Files

Scope and Content note

Series I: Production Files is comprised of paper archives that relate to Collective: Unconscious's production of theatrical events. This series includes scripts for individual plays and festivals, proposals, publicity, and miscellaneous papers associated with the general production of artistic forms and materials relating to the rental of performance space. Also included are near-comprehensive show reports documenting the date, title, attendance and ticket price of various performances. The material has been arranged within four subseries:

Subseries A: General
Subseries B: Programs, Press, and Promotional
Subseries C: Scripts
Subseries D: Publications/Source Material

Series II: Administrative Files

Scope and Content note

Series II: Administrative Files includes documents relating to building maintenance, day-to-day operations and communication. Extensive billing records and itemized receipts from multiple operating years are also included. This series also contains meeting minutes, financial records, tax returns and grant paperwork. Among the financial records are copies of the group's leases and lengthy correspondence with both landlords and attorneys over issues and disputes. Of particular interest are folders relevant to the lease and to insurance, which document the increasing cost of rental and fire insurance in a Lower East Side neighborhood in a state of rapid change after 9/11. The material has been divided into three subseries:

Subseries A: General
Subseries B: Minutes
Subseries C: Financial
