Series III: Audio Recordings

Scope and Content

This series contains all audio recordings stored on magnetic audiocassettes. It includes live performances at Darinka, compilation tapes, demo tapes, and miscellaneous recordings organized alphabetically by artist or project title.

Conditions Governing Access

Audiovisual materials have not been preserved and may not be available to researchers.
