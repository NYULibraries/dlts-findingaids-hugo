Series I: Slides, 1970-2004

Scope and Contents

Series I consists of slides for several of Winslow's projects including "Democracy in America", "Cost of Living", and "Post War", plus slides of swans, flowers, clouds, individuals, architecture, objects, and locations in the United States and Europe.

Series II: Photographs, 1960-2003

Extent

1.5 Linear Feet

Scope and Contents

Series II contains color and black and white photos of and by Kirk Winslow, including Winslow's copyrighted 1980s photos of Manhattan music club CBGB and performance artist Tim Miller. Also here are photos from Europe and New York City neighborhoods and buildings, plus photos of interiors and furnishings as well as some abstract photos.

Series III: Contact Sheets, 1970-1995

Extent

.5 Linear Feet

Scope and Contents

Series III contains contact sheets, most unmarked and undated, for black and white shots of Manhattan locations, interiors, individuals drama performances, and performance artists. Some sheets are titled "Sunspot" by John Jesurun, the Kitchen, NYC, March 1989. Others are of performance artist Stuart Sherman.
