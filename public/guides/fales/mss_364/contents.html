31142054880250box: 6, folder: 25 (Material Type: Mixed Materials)

Abstract

Riot Grrrl [Vancouver] no. 5

Writings in this zine critique mainstream ideals of beauty, express nostalgia for youth and girlhood, question established and traditional careers and lifestyles, and talk about revolution. Other themes include equality in sexual relationships and frustration with them, straight edge and vegan advocacy, and danger signals to watch for in men. A word search, natural and homeopathic health remedies, excerpts from famous female writers concerning men, a David Wojnarowicz piece, and zine lists and reviews are included. Discussions on riot grrrl include different forms of the movement, reactions to stereotypes, and chapter information.
