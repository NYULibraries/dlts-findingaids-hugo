Abstract

Adele Fournet is a composer, musician, video artist, and music producer based in Brooklyn, NY. The Adele Fournet Papers contain born digital materials created between 2008-2017 documenting Fournet's career and musical works. The collection includes audio and video files, an inventory spreadsheet and an archived copy of her website, adelefournet.com. The collection documents her solo work as well as her collaborations with other artists.

Biographical Note

Adele Fournet is a musician, composer, music producer and video artist born in Fayetteville, AR and based in Brooklyn, NY. She works both as a solo artist and in collaborations with other artists.

Scope and Contents

The Adele Fournet Papers contain born-digital materials created between 2008-2017 documenting Fournet's career and musical works. The collection includes audio and video files, an inventory spreadsheet, and an archived copy of her website, adelefournet.com. The collection documents her solo work as well as her collaborations with other artists in projects such as The Soon-Another and a string quartet. The website contains a short biography of the artist, a list of texts and scores, documentary videos, music and composition videos, and audio files of her works and performances. The website documents her work as a musician, producer, video artist, and composer, who has been involved as a percussionist, vocalist, and keyboard player in several ensembles in addition to composing.

One hard drive was forensically imaged, analyzed, and arranged in Forensic Toolkit. Adele Fournet's website was archived to the Internet Archive using the Archive-It service. In 2020, additional archived websites were added to the web archive.

New York University Libraries follows professional standards and best practices when imaging, ingesting, and processing born-digital material in order to maintain the integrity of the content.

Arrangement

Custodial History

Websites are selected by curators and captured through the use of Archive-It. Archive-It uses web crawling technology to capture websites at a scheduled time and displays only an archived copy, from the resulting WARC file, of the website.

Physical Characteristics and Technical Requirements

Advance notice is required for the use of computer records. Original physical digital media is restricted.

Due to technical or privacy issues, archived websites may not be exact copies of the original website at the time of the web crawl. Certain file types will not be captured dependent on how they are embedded in the site. Other parts of websites that the crawler has difficulty capturing includes Javascript, streaming content, database-driven content, and highly interactive content. Full-Text searches of archived websites are available at https://archive-it.org/organizations/567.
