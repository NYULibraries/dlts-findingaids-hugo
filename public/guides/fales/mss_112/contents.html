Series I: Original Works (Oversize)

Arrangement note

Materials are divided by format into two Subseries (Drawings and Animation cels), and arranged alphabetically by title within each subseries.

Scope and Content

This series contains all of the original artworks in the archive. Subseries A features a particularly strong collection of drawings from The Secret of Nimh (1982). Strengths of Subseries B include animation cels from Thumbelina (1994). Some of the animation cels are signed by Don Bluth (see notes below item descriptions).

Series III: Drafts and Story Boards

Scope and Content

This series contains story boards from Rocket Power (1999), a television series on which DeMoura served as story artist, as well as script drafts of a number of Disney films, including Dumbo (1941) and Alice in Wonderland (1951).
