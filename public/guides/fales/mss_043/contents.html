Series II: Manuscripts

Scope and Contents note

Series II contains the mss. of Paulson's translations of Henrik Ibsen, August Strindberg and other Scandinavian authors. Subseries A contains Paulson's translations of Ibsen, as well as miscellaneous Ibsen ephemera collected by Paulson; the ephemera includes playbills, postcards and criticism. Series II, Subseries B contains Paulson's mss. translations of Strindberg; subseries C contains the same material, this time in drafts prepared for publication. Subseries D contains Paulson's translations of Vilhelm Moberg, Knut Hamsun, Gustav Geijerstam and other Scandinavian authors. Subseries E contains miscellaneous manuscripts.

Scope and Contents note

Series III contains criticism and translations published by Paulson and other writers in Swedish and American periodicals. Subseries A contains Paulson's articles on Strindberg, as well as Paulson's translations of Strindberg, Selma Lagerlof and Arvid Skappel. Subseries B contains articles on Strindberg and on Strindberg studies by other authors, as well as a 1913 translation of Strindberg's "Half a Sheet of Paper.

Series IV: Printed Matter and Memorabilia

Scope and Contents note

Series IV contains playbills, photographs, tickets, press clippings and other material from Paulson's life as a producer, performer and theater-goer; in addition it contains publicity material for his books and misc. Strindberg memorabilia. Subseries A contains playbills, photographs and tickets from performances of Strindberg's plays in Sweden and America, 1918-1979; Paulson either acted in or supplied the translations for some of these productions. Subseries A also contains misc. material from Paulson's life in the theatre, such as a 1941 manual for theatre staff and an assortment of actors' periodicals. Subseries B contains Strindberg memorabilia.
