Series I: Correspondence

Scope and Contents note

This series consists of Terence Sellers' personal and business related correspondence from the 1970s through the 1990s, and includes snapshots, small mixed media drawings and paintings, and various printed materials. There is extensive correpondence between Sellers and Genesis P-Orridge, and Duncan Smith. The series also contains correspondence between Sellers and Kathy Acker, and others. Materials are organized chronologically by date, grouped by person or subject. Many folders include both correspondence to and from Sellers.

Series IV: Writing

Scope and Contents note

Series IV: Writings is divided into five subseries according to genre. Subseries A contains Sellers' short stories, poems and plays, organized alphabetically. Subseries B includes Sellers' novels, organized alphabetically by title. These materials also include research for each project including notes, manuscripts, drafts, proofs, and promotion and publication materials. Subseries C contains original artwork by Jeffrey Bretschneider and Carl Apfelschnitt that was produced to illustrate editions of her published work. Subseries D is a scrapbook that Sellers created of clippings from magazines and newspapers relating to her life and literary career. Subseries E includes copies of Sellers' published essays and prose works, including various editions of works. Original folders are also included with most of the work in this series because in many instances Sellers took notes and brainstormed on her original folders. All work is arranged alphabetically by project.
