About Itself, 1968-1995

31142063577202box: 1, folder: 1 (Material Type: mixed materials)

Guitar and cello part to a fourteen-measure composition, not in Hemphill's hand.

Abstrac, 1968-1995

31142063577202box: 1, folder: 2 (Material Type: mixed materials)

Scope and Contents

Nineteen-page holograph score, ruled by hand, orchestrated for a jazz big band (five woodwinds, three trumpets, French horn, trombone, tuba, cello, bass, and percussion).

Affairs of the Heart, 1980-1995

31142063577202box: 1, folder: 3 (Material Type: mixed materials)

Scope and Contents

Professionally copied parts to a work for saxophone quartet, orchestrated using woodwind doubles (soprano saxophone, flute, alto flute, bass clarinet).

After The Crowd, 1978-1995

31142063577202box: 1, folder: 4 (Material Type: mixed materials)

Scope and Contents

Five-page score photocopied from Sightation Notebook for a work for clarinet, French horn, saxophone, bass, and drums. Sightation Notebook includes an ordered list of the suite of compositions with circus-themed titles, from which this piece is drawn.

Ahmad the Terrible, 1986-1995

31142063577202box: 1, folder: 5 (Material Type: mixed materials)

Scope and Contents

Holograph concert score. Sixty-three-measure big-band arrangement of a Jack DeJohnette composition dedicated to pianist Ahmad Jamal. The folder also includes a holograph score for backgrounds during the solo section from measure 63, with detailed notes to the copyist in red ink. The melody of the original DeJohnette composition is not notated in the score.

Air Rings, 1968-1995

31142063577244box: 5, folder: 1 (Material Type: mixed materials)

Scope and Contents

Score in holograph for a work in two voices in treble and bass clef. There is a treble melody line, first designated for flute, but then suggested for soprano saxophone 8vb. The bass clef part is mostly single notes, though double stops may indicate a cello. There are also professionally copied soprano saxophone and bass parts, as well as parts in holograph for trumpet and guitar. The piece has an eight-bar intro, a twenty-bar A section, repeated, and an eight-bar bridge.

Anchorman - from "Last Supper", 1990-1990

31142063577202box: 1, folder: 6 (Material Type: mixed materials)

Scope and Contents

Photocopy of professionally copied parts for saxophone sextet (two soprano, alto, two tenor, and baritone), based on material from "The Last Supper at Uncle Tom's Cabin / The Promised Land."

Anchorman - Theatrical Piece, 1968-1995

31142063577202box: 1, folder: 7 (Material Type: mixed materials)

Scope and Contents

Holograph score in pencil of a piece for trumpet, two tenor saxophones, and bass clarinet. Score is in five lines, with meticulous phrase markings. Pages 4 and 5 (of 10 indicated) are missing. This piece was written for "Anchorman," a theatrical work in two acts by Paul Carter Harrison. Folder also includes a shorter score, marked "Act I," orchestrated for soprano saxophone, trumpet, tenor saxophone, and bass clarinet.

Another Feeling, 1981-1995

31142063577244box: 5, folder: 2 (Material Type: mixed materials)

Scope and Contents

Professionally copied parts for a saxophone quartet (soprano, alto, tenor, and baritone). Composition is part of a suite entitled "Wind/Rhythms" written for World Saxophone Quartet's collaboration with Max Roach's M'Boom. This work is a slow chorale. See "Wind/Rhythms" for full score.

Answer, The, 1991-1995

31142063577244box: 5, folder: 3 (Material Type: mixed materials)

Scope and Contents

Transposed holograph score in pencil of a piece for saxophone sextet (soprano, two altos, two tenors, baritone). Twenty-one measures. Hemphill wrote this work as a concert piece for the Julius Hemphill Saxophone Sextet; folder includes set of manuscript parts not in composer's hand and professionally copied parts use for the Sextet folio published by Subito Music.

Antecedent, 1977-1995

31142063577202box: 1, folder: 8 (Material Type: mixed materials)

Scope and Contents

Photocopy of "Fourth Blues: Uttered Flute" (a title in a decorative compartment) from Foran Notebook. An improvised flute solo, this piece was included on the album Blue Boyé (1977) under the title "Antecedent."

Ascension, 1990-1995

31142063577244box: 5, folder: 4 (Material Type: mixed materials)

Scope and Contents

Professionally copied parts to a piece for saxophone sextet (two sopranos, alto, two tenors, baritone) originally written for "Last Supper at Uncle Tom's Cabin / The Promised Land."

Astrid, 1968-1995

31142063577202box: 1, folder: 9 (Material Type: mixed materials)

Scope and Contents

Holograph score in pencil for an up-tempo piece orchestrated for soprano saxophone, guitar, bass, and drums. It is eighteen measures long with a written tag; the form is AABA + tag. Score is annotated with phone numbers and addresses for different individuals. Folder also includes professionally copied parts.

At Dr. King's Table, 1990-1995

31142063577202box: 1, folder: 10 (Material Type: mixed materials)

Scope and Contents

Professionally copied parts for saxophone sextet (soprano, two alto, two tenor, and baritone). See "Last Supper at Uncle Tom's Cabin / The Promised Land" for additional score and parts.

31142063577202box: 1, folder: 11 (Material Type: mixed materials)

Holograph score for a work for alto saxophone and cello. Folder also includes a score in holograph for alto saxophone, guitar, bass, and drums, with detailed notes to the copyist, as well as a holograph alto saxophone part and a photocopy of a trumpet part from Trumpet Notebook.

Band Theme, 1993-1995

31142063577244box: 5, folder: 6 (Material Type: mixed materials)

Scope and Contents

Holograph score in pencil for saxophone sextet (soprano, two altos, two tenors, baritone), titled "Backgrounds Band Theme." This piece was written for Julius Hemphill Sextet and was included in "Long Tongues: A Saxophone Opera." Parts were improvised during the tenor saxophone solo based on Jimmy Heath's "CTA" chord changes.

Basso / Pro Bird, 1968-1995

31142063577244box: 5, folder: 7 (Material Type: mixed materials)

Scope and Contents

Score with professionally-copied parts for a piece for flute, bass, and drums. Vellum and light-sensitive paper. Includes note for copyist that a guitar part should be made. The piece is eighteen measures long, with intro in 7/4 and main section in 2/2. Hemphill's notes indicate that a portion of this composition is used in the eighth movement of the "Drunk on God" suite. That movement is titled "Mailika" on the album Julius Hemphill Big Band (1988); it is titled "Maliaka: Drum / Woman" in Hemphill's score.

Bayou Sketch, 1977-1995

31142063577202box: 1, folder: 13 (Material Type: mixed materials)

Scope and Contents

Four parts in holograph for saxophone quartet (soprano, alto, tenor, baritone). Included on the album Point of No Return (1977) as "Bajou Scetches" (and attributed to Hamiet Bluiett).

Big Band, 1968-1995

31142063577202box: 1, folder: 14 (Material Type: mixed materials)

Big Drum Speaks, The, 1988-1995

31142063577202box: 1, folder: 15 (Material Type: mixed materials)

Scope and Contents

Parts in holograph for two guitars and bass (the instrumentation of Hemphill's JAH band), with chord symbols in a different hand. The parts are from a movement in the suite "Drunk on God," which appears on the album Julius Hemphill Big Band (1988). The movement is titled "Cosmic Country Boy" on the recording. See "Drunk on God" for parts and score, with title sometimes as "From Out of Nowhere: The Big Drum Speaks."

Bitter Glory, A: Opening Song, 1994-1995

31142063577202box: 1, folder: 18 (Material Type: mixed materials)

Scope and Contents

Two-page holograph score for a piece with a soprano or alto vocal line and unorchestrated accompaniment. In pencil and black ink with annotations in red ink; photocopies include further annotations. Several manuscript notebooks include parts for this piece for reeds/woodwinds (soprano and alto saxophone, clarinet, flute), brass (trumpet, French horn, trombone), cello, and bass. The piece is the "Opening Song" from music written for the theater piece "A Bitter Glory."

31142063577202box: 1, folder: 20 (Material Type: mixed materials)

Bordertown (San Antonio), 1980-1995

31142063577202box: 1, folder: 21 (Material Type: mixed materials)

Scope and Contents

Holograph score in pencil, with annotations in red ink, of a big-band arrangement dated 1980. There are further additions in Hemphill's hand, with background parts and harmonizations corresponding to the album Julius Hemphill Big Band (1988). The folder also includes a saxophone quartet arrangement (soprano, soprano/alto, bass clarinet / tenor saxophone, alto clarinet / baritone saxophone), as well as professionally copied parts; the first page (of two) for the bass clarinet / tenor saxophone part is missing. Folder includes a piano score in holograph for "Bordertown," with the the annotation "Good Boye Music ASCAP" on each page.

Changes, 1968-1995

31142063577202box: 1, folder: 31 (Material Type: mixed materials)

Scope and Contents

Photocopy of score, possibly in Hemphill's hand, with written instructions in Hemphill's hand, for flute, baritone saxophone, cello, and drums. The piece may be incomplete, as there is a letter "D" following the fermata in measure 46.

Children's Song, The - Saxophone quartet version, 1975-1995
