Series I: Mail Art/Correspondence, 1976-2006

Dates:
1976-2006

Scope and Content

This series comprises the bulk of the collection and contains mail art sent back and forth between Mark Bloch and thousands of artists in all 50 states and dozens of countries. The series includes mail art in myriad forms: letters, postcards, zines, pamphlets, enclosures, collages, drawings, and paintings, among other media.

Arrangement

This series has been arranged into three subseries by format:

Subseries A: Correspondence [General Mail Art]
Subseries B: Postcards
Subseries C: Empty Envelopes

Subseries A is arranged alphabetically by geographical place name, based on the postmark of the piece of mail art. Subseries B and C are arranged by size.
