Abstract

David Giannini is an American poet who has published more than 30 collections with small presses, and whose work has appeared in national and international literary magazines and anthologies. Opens, his first book of poetry, was published in 1971 by Genesis: Grasp Press, which he edited and co-founded in 1967. The David Giannini Collection on Richard Hell and Genesis: Grasp (dated 1968-1972) consists of correspondence between Richard Hell and Giannini, the majority of which consists of letters from Hell.

Scope and Contents

The David Giannini Collection on Richard Hell and Genesis: Grasp (dated 1968-1972) consists of correspondence between Richard Hell and David Giannini, an early collaborator on projects such as the poetry journal Genesis: Grasp. The majority of the collection consists of letters from Hell.
