Abstract

The Coalition for Student Rights at New York University (NYU) was formed in 1981 to seek greater student participation in institutional decision making at NYU. This collection consists of organization materials, including newsletters, a bill of rights, and student surveys.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Donated by the Coalition for Student Rights in 1993.

Historical Note

The Coalition for Student Rights at New York University (NYU) was formed in 1981 to seek greater student participation in institutional decision making at NYU. Specifically, the coalition sought more student representation on decision making bodies and greater involvement in grading policies, budget formulation, financial aid decisions, faculty and staff hiring, student housing, minority recruitment, and placement.

Scope and Contents

This collection consists of organizational records of the Coalition for Student Rights at NYU. Record types include newsletter galleys and articles, flyers, and survey forms.
