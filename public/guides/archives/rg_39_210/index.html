Abstract

This collection contains issues from the student newspaper the Heights Daily Journal, dating from 1933 to 1973. This newspaper documented student life at NYU (particuarly at the University Heights campus in the Bronx), local and national news, campus living, and student clubs. NYU's University College of Arts and Science and the School of Engineering were located in the University Heights neighborhood of the Bronx from 1894 to 1973.

Historical Note

New York University has had many student newspapers since its founding. The University Heights campus and the Washington Square campus had separate student newspapers until 1973 when the University Heights campus closed.
