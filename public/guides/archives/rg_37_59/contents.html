Extent

2 websites in two archived websites.

Scope and Contents

Website that documents events, projects, and publications from the Center for Religion and Media at New York University. The Center is jointly supported by the Department of Anthropology and the Department of Religious Studies and works closely with the Center for Media, Culture, and History at NYU. The collection also includes their digital publication the Revealer, which publishes essays, investigative journalism, opinion pieces, reviews, interviews, and photography that reflects on religion through race, sexuality, gender, politics, history, and culture.

Accruals

New site crawls are accrued annually.

Appraisal

Crawl was limited to domains and subdomains of wp.nyu.edu/crm, therevealer.org, wp.nyu.edu/cmch, s18798.pcdn.co, and wp.nyu.edu/therevealer in order to remain within the collection scope and data constraints.

Physical Characteristics and Technical Requirements

Due to technical or privacy issues, archived websites may not be exact copies of the original website at the time of the web crawl. Certain file types will not be captured dependent on how they are embedded in the site. Other parts of websites that the crawler has difficulty capturing includes Javascript, streaming content, database-driven content, and highly interactive content. Full-Text searches of archived websites are available at https://archive-it.org/organizations/567.
