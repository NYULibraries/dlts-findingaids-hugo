Abstract

Jeffrey Leeds is an actor, producer, and alumnus of New York University with a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology and Master of Science in Public Administration. He was a member of the NYU Glee Club and the original Vice President of the NYU Choral Arts Alumni Club. The Jeffrey Leeds Papers (dated 1936-2000) consist of materials created and collected by Jeffrey Leeds documenting his participation in the New York University Glee Club and other aspects of the university's musical organizations. Materials include performance programs, audio recordings, photographs, and other ephemera. The collection also includes Leeds' Glee Club blazer.

Content Description

The Jeffrey Leeds Papers (dated 1936-2000) consist of materials created and collected by Jeffrey Leeds documenting his participation in the New York University Glee Club and other aspects of the university's musical organizations. Materials include performance programs, audio recordings, photographs, and other ephemera. The collection also includes Leeds' Glee Club blazer and 11 photographs taken by Arthur Smithline documenting student musical group activities in the 1930s.

Processing Information

At the time of accessioning materials were placed in acid-free folders and boxes, and described on the collection- and item-level. No other arrangement or physical interventions have taken place.

The collection's audio recordings have been digitized.

Arrangement

This collection has not been arranged by an archivist. The materials are arranged in the order in which they were received from the donor.

Custodial History

Photographs taken by Arthur Smithline were donated to E. Jon Revere by Suzanne Smithline Manion, Smithline's daughter, and her husband Glenn Manion. Revere, a former conductor of the NYU Glee Club, later gave the photographs to Jeffrey Leeds to be added to his collection.
