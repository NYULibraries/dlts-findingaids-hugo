Abstract

George Schwartz received his Bachelor of Science with honors in biology from New York University (NYU) in 1925. This collection contains materials relating to undergraduate biology courses in which he was enrolled 1921-1925, such as histology and embryology, as well as his honors thesis and various other lab work and papers.

Biographical Note

George Schwartz received his Bachelor of Science with honors in biology from New York University (NYU) in 1925. He graduated from Bellevue Medical College in 1929. While at NYU, he participated in activities such as the Politics Club, the Menorah Society, the Junior Show, the Paint and Powder Club, and the Stuyvesant Club.

Scope and Content Note

This collection consists of one box containing class materials from George Schwartz's undergraduate biology classes 1921-1925. Consisting mostly of handwritten lecture notes, it includes other materials, such as lab work and typed papers. Also included is his 1925 honors thesis.
