Abstract

Edmund V. Bragdon, a graduate of New York University (NYU), collected memorabilia related to his time as a student at NYU. Materials include event programs, dance cards, and a photograph of students taken in 1904.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Materials donated by Virginia Bragdon Easton in circa 1997. The accession number associated with this collection is 97.004.
