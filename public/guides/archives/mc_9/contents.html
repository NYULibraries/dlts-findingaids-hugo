Series VI: Ocean County Schools, 1893-1902

langmaterial

English

Scope and Contents

Materials from North's activities as principal of Toms River High School (1893-1896) and as superintendent of the Ocean County Schools (1897-1902); included are hand-copied student newspapers, 1894-1896; reports of the superintendent, 1897-1902; and letter books used as texts written by North while traveling abroad, 1900-1901.
