Abstract

Herman Emil Mantel (d. 1964) was a multi-degree graduate of New York University, earning his Bachelor of Arts degree in 1908, Master of Arts degree in 1909, and PhD in 1916. He went on to teach and become an administator at Bronx High School of Science. This collection contains 1908 class day booklets and a commencement program, a 1909 Masters in Arts diploma, a 1916 commencement program, and undated photograph of Mantel.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Biographical Note

Herman Emil Mantel (d. 1964) graduated from New York University with a Bachelor of Arts degree in 1908, Master of Arts degree in 1909, and PhD in 1916. Mantel taught and was an administrator at the Bronx High School of Science. The Herman E. Mantel Library was dedicated at the Bronx High School of Science in 1966.
