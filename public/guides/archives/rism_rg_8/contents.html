Series III. Indexes, 1964-1967

Extent

127.3 Linear Feet in 7 card boxes and 156 shoe boxes

Scope and Contents

This series consists of index cards that serve as a quick reference to the original field notes. The Community Study Outline Indexed Field Notes cards were created from photocopies of the field notes that were organized according to coded topics and then cut and pasted onto cards, arranged according to the coded topic. The Biographical Index Cards contain the names of individuals living in each community, their ages, occupations, and place of residence.

Arrangement

This series has been arranged into two subseries according to type of index: Subseries A. Biographical Index Card and Subseries B. Community Study Outline Indexed Field Notes Card. Cards in the subseries are arranged by community name.
