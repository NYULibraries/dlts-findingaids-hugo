Abstract

Beverly Kaplan graduated in 1948 from the New York University School of Commerce, Accounts and Finance. The Beverly (Beldner) Kaplan Papers consist of materials created and collected while Kaplan was a student at the School of Commerce, Accounts and Finance from 1944 to 1948. The collection includes photographs of Kaplan with her fellow classmates, a diploma, report cards, an engraved fountain pen and mechanical pencil with her name, a class yearbook, and a New York University alumna card.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Biographical Note

Beverly Kaplan graduated in 1948 from New York University the School of Commerce, Accounts and Finance. Kaplan passed away in February 2017.
