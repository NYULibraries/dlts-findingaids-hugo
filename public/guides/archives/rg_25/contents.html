Series IV. Accreditation Records, 1973-2003

langmaterial

English

Extent

16.5 Linear Feet in 16 record cartons and 1 manuscript box

Scope and Contents

This series consists of materials created through the course of a reaccreditation process that document various administrative and educational aspects of the College of Dentistry's programs. Materials include syllabi, information about the curriculum of the programs, reports outlining the programs' compliance with accreditation standards, and materials produced in preparation for accreditation site visits.
