Scope and Contents

This collection consists of a 1922 Physiology Lab textbook, entitled "Directions for Classwork in Experimental Physiology - Stein." It was used by Arthur A. Blum, a student at the New York College of Dentistry in 1924. The book has extensive manuscript diagrams, annotations and marginalia, along with tipped in kymograph readings. A few items of ephemera were laid in behind the front cover. These include a 1921 Bulletin from the NYC Deparment of Health, a Syllabus of Bacteriology, and several more kymograph readings.
