Series I: General NYU Material, 1930-1973

langmaterial

English

Extent

0.3 Linear Feet in one manuscript box

Scope and Contents

Series I includes Hart's statement on NYU's Program for Leadership and Service in the 1960s; correspondence related to the Hall of Fame, the Institute of Mechanical Engineers, and the Katra-Black Student Group; proposals written in 1965 related to funding the construction of a technology building at the University Heights Campus; a 1952 survey of the University Heights Library; a 1965 survey on the total data processing requirements of NYU; and pamphlets and clippings related to University Heights campus facilities and general university activities.
