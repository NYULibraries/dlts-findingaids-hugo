Abstract

Dr. Joseph T. Hart served as the curator and librarian at the Gould Memorial Library on the University Heights campus. The collection includes correspondence, newspaper clippings, publications, and reports regarding New York University and the School of Engineering and Science from 1930-1973.

Scope and Contents

This collection is composed of materials collected by Dr. Joseph T. Hart regarding New York University and the School of Engineering and Science from 1930-1973. Materials include correspondence related to the Hall of Fame, the Institute of Medical Engineers, and the Katra-Black Student Group; newspaper clippings related to general university activities, and construction and opening of the Engineering library at the University Heights campus; pamphlets related to facilities funding and University Heights campus buildings; reports related to the total data processing requirements for NYU in 1965; proposals for facility funding; and a statement written by Hart in 1961 related to NYU's Program for Leadership and Services in the 1960s.
