Archived Websites

Dates:
March 2019-November 2019
box: Electronic Records (Material Type: electronic records)

Extent

3 websites in three archived websites.

Scope and Contents

These web sites document the University's participation in a city-wide celebration of the 50th anniversary of the Stonewall Riots. It contains a listing of events, the "Stonewall at 50" documentary, videos of past events, a timeline of NYU and the LGBTQ+ community, and information on arts exhibits, lectures, film series, operas, and events at NYU study abroad sites. It also includes the Liberal Studies Program's website on its events series to commemorate the Stonewall Riots and a press release on the AIDS Memorial Quilt display in the Mamdouha Bobst Gallery.

Accruals

New site crawls are accrued monthly.

Appraisal

Crawl was limited to domains and subdomains of nyu.edu/about/university-initiatives/nyu-celebrates-stonewall-at-50, liberalstudies.nyu.edu/news-events/2019/stonewall-50, and nyu.edu/about/news-publications/news/2019/september/nyu-to-display-aids-memorial-quilt-for-stonewall-50th-anniversar.html in order to remain within the collection scope and data constraints.

Physical Characteristics and Technical Requirements

Due to technical or privacy issues, archived websites may not be exact copies of the original website at the time of the web crawl. Certain file types will not be captured dependent on how they are embedded in the site. Other parts of websites that the crawler has difficulty capturing includes Javascript, streaming content, database-driven content, and highly interactive content. Full-Text searches of archived websites are available at https://archive-it.org/organizations/567.
