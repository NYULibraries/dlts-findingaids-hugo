Abstract

This collection contains issues from the student newspaper the Education Sun, dating from 1931 to 1955. The paper was published by NYU School of Education students (later known as the Steinhardt School of Culture, Education, and Human Development), and documented campus life, local and national news, athletics, and other student activities. The collection is extensive but not comprehensive, there may be some issues that were published but are not included in this collection. This collections includes issues in their original print format as well as microfilmed copies.

Historical Note

New York University has had many student newspapers since its founding. The Education Sun was the student newspaper for the NYU School of Education (later known as the Steinhardt School of Culture, Education, and Human Development) from 1931 through 1955.
