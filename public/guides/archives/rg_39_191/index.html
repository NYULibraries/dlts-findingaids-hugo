Abstract

This collection contains issues from the Washington Square College Evening Division's student newspaper dating from 1926 to 1967. It documents local and national news, campus living, student clubs, and student life at NYU as it pertained to the Evening Division students. The newspaper was published under three titles: Guess (1926), Nite News (1927-1932), and Washington Square College Evening News (1933-1967). The collection is extensive but not comprehensive, there may be some issues that were published but are not included in this collection. This collection includes issues in their original print format as well as microfilmed copies. Some issues are bound.

Historical Note

New York University has had many student newspapers with many different names since its founding. The Heights campus and the Washington Square campus had separate student newspapers until 1973 when the Heights campus was closed. The Evening Division was the designation for students who took night classes at Washington Square College. The newspaper was published under three titles: Guess (1926), Nite News (1927-1932), and Washington Square College Evening News (1933-1967).
