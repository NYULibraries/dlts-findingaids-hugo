Abstract

William Chidekel was enrolled in a Master of Arts program at New York University in the early 1950s. This collection consists of Chidekel's dissertation, "Living Accommodations in New York City, As Described by Contemporaries, 1780-1960," submitted in partial fulfillment of the requirements for the degree of Master of Arts at New York University in June 1951.
