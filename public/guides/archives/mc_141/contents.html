Scope and Contents note

Includes general correspondence to and from Slavson from 1930-1978.Correspondence concerns books and papers written by him, letters to editors, requests for information from social service agencies worldwide and letters to and from family members and friends. Also included are invitations to lecture, lecture schedules and biographical material,including newspaper and magazine clippings and speeches from his retirement dinner at the Jewish Board of Guardians.
