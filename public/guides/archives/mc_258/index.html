Abstract

The collection contains ledgers recording the accession of books, periodicals, manuscripts, and other items by various New York University libraries between 1830 and 1967. Some ledgers contain catalogs of authors, theses, and bill payments.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Materials found in collection; there is no documentation concerning the provenance of these materials.

Scope and Contents

The collection contains ledgers recording the accession of books, periodicals, manuscripts, and other items by various New York University libraries between 1830 and 1967. Some ledgers contain catalogs of authors, theses, and bill payments.
