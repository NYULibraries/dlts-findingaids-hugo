Abstract

Ida Elizabeth Kopp was a night student at New York University's School of Commerce, Accounts and Finance (later known as the Leonard N. Stern School of Business) from approximately 1926 through 1932. She was the only daughter of an immigrant couple from Germany and Switzerland. She graduated with a Bachelor's degree and later worked as a secretary to the President of Stauffer Chemical Company located in the Graybar Building of Grand Central Station. This collection consists of Kopp's student notebook from her classes at NYU from roughly 1927 through 1932.
