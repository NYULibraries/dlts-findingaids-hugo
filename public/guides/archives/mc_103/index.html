Abstract

Ira Hutchinson Brainerd was an alumnus of New York University, member of the Psi Upsilon Fraternity, and author. This collection consists of his scrapbook of newspaper clippings and other documents related to his time at NYU.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Materials found in collection; there is no documentation concerning the provenance of these materials.
