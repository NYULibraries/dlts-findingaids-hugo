Abstract

This collection contains issues from the student newspaper the Square Daily dating from April to May 1953. This newspaper documented student life at NYU (particuarly at the Washington Square campus), local and national news, campus living, and student clubs. The collection is extensive but not comprehensive, there may be some issues that were published but are not included in this collection.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Materials found in collection; there is no documentation concerning the provenance of these materials.

Historical Note

New York University has had many student newspapers since its founding. The University Heights campus and the Washington Square campus had separate student newspapers until 1973 when the University Heights campus was closed.

Scope and Contents

This collection contains issues of student newspaper the Square Daily from 1953. he newspaper was printed daily and documented student life (particularly at the Washington Square campus), university news, and local or international news with relevancy to NYU.
