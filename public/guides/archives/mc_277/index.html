Abstract

Waldo E. Chapin (1917 - 2011) graduated from New York University with a Degree of Bachelor of Science in February 1948. The collection contains Chapin's final examination portfolio for his Introduction to Art course, a graduation confirmation letter, and a 1948 commencement program.

Biographical / Historical

Waldo Chapin (1917 - 2011) graduated from New York University with a Degree of Bachelor of Science in February 1948. Chapin was a Staff Sergeant in the U.S. Army Air Corps where he served from 1939-1945. He worked at the National Council on Compensation Insurance (NCCI) for forty years.
