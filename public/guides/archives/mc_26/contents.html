Series I: Correspondence, 1900-1986

langmaterial

English

Scope and Contents

The "Correspondence" series is extremely comprehensive, and includes letters to Katsh from academics and notables in the field of Hebrew and/or Judaic studies, largely from Israel or America. There are also a number of letters to Katsh from representatives of the Jewish community in the Soviet Union. This series is arranged chronologically.

Conditions Governing Access

Material pertaining to individual student records may be restricted in accordance with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA). Restricted materials have been moved to separate folders within the box and are noted as restricted.

Series II: Articles and Clippings , 1932-1982

langmaterial

English

Scope and Contents

The "Articles and Clippings' series consists mainly of articles written by Dr. Katsh for newspapers and scholarly journals. Articles written by others about Katsh were preserved in Katsh's scrapbooks, along with a limited amount of correspondence.

Conditions Governing Access

Material pertaining to individual student records may be restricted in accordance with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA). Restricted materials have been moved to separate folders within the box and are noted as restricted.

langmaterial

Scope and Contents

The "scrapbooks" series consists of scrapbooks that Katsh created to document his career and achievements. The scrapbooks contain mostly newspaper clippings, many of which are reviews of Katsh's books, as well as a few pieces of correspondence and articles published by Katsh.
