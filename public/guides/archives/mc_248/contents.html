Extent

Scope and Contents

Series I includes invitations and programs for events hosted by the university or groups associated with it, including the military honor society Scabbard and Blade, the ROTC program, and the Engineering Societies. Other materials include assorted issues of the New York University Medley, a student magazine, NYU football ticket stubs and programs
