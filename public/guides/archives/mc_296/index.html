Abstract

The Harry R. Gudenberg Collection contains a 1957-1958 NYU Log booklet, a "1961 NYU Frosh" pin, and a program for the 1958 NYU School of Commerce, Accounts, and Finance Evening Freshman Orientation event. Gudenberg was a student at NYU, serving as the Junior Class Secretary-Treasurer for the Evening Student Council in 1958.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Donated by Henry Gudenberg in August 2018. The accession number associated with this gift is 2019.071.

Biographical Note

Harry R. Gudenberg was born in 1933. He graduated with a Bachelor of Science from New York University in 1960 and received his Master of Business Administration from New York University in 1964. He went on to receive a Juris Doctor from Seton Hall University in 1970. He has worked as an arbitrator and mediator.

Scope and Contents

This collection contains Henry R. Gudenberg's 1958 NYU School of Commerce, Accounts, and Finance Evening Freshman Orientation program of events, a 1957-1958 NYU Log booklet which provided in-coming students with basic school information and activities, and a "1961 NYU Frosh" pin.
