Abstract

Robert Ewing Anthony, Jr. graduated from the University of the City of New-York (later New York University) in 1883. The collection comprises his student notebook, dated January 1883.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

The provenance of this collection is unknown. The accession number associated with this collection is 87.008.

Biographical Note

Robert Ewing Anthony, Jr. attended the University of the City of New-York from 1879 to 1883. He was enrolled in the Department of Arts and Science. From 1879 to 1883, the Catalogue of the University of the City of New-York listed his home in Brooklyn. Anthony graduated from the University of the City of New-York in 1883.

Scope and Contents

This collection contains the student notebook of Robert Ewing Anthony, Jr. The notebook is titled: "Notes Volume II: Statics Dynamics Hydraulics." According to Anthony, the notes are based on notes of Professor Gillespie of Union College, rearranged and corrected by Professor Charles B. Brush, professor of Civil Engineering at New York University. Additionally, Brush added extracts from "Merrill's Iron Truss bridges" and other data obtained from the investigation, experience, and practice of Spielman and Brush, Civil Engineers. The notebook is dated January 1883.
