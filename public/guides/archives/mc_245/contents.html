Series I: Postcards, circa 1930s

langmaterial

English

Scope and Contents

Series I includes postcards depicting New York City landmarks as they appeared in the 1930s. Landmarks that are particularly well documented in the collection include the city's churches, bridges, museums, hotels, and theaters, as well as the Chrysler Building, Empire State Building, Rockefeller Center, and the Washington Square Arch.

Series II: Photographs, circa 1930s

langmaterial

English

Scope and Contents

Series II includes a small number of photographs presumably taken by Otness during the same time period. Their subject matter is largely similar to that of the postcards in the collection: Washington Square Park is heavily featured, as is the RMS Queen Mary (which completed its first transatlantic voyage to New York in 1936), and the Macy's Thanksgiving Day Parade.
