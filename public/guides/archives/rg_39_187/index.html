Abstract

This collection contains issues from the student newspaper the Square Journal, later known as the Washington Square Journal, dating from 1955 to 1973. This newspaper documented student life at NYU (particuarly at the Washington Square campus), local and national news, campus living, and student clubs. The collection is extensive but not comprehensive, there may be some issues that were published but are not included in this collection. This collections includes issues in their original print format as well as microfilmed copies.

Biographical / Historical

New York University has had many student newspapers since its founding. The University Heights campus and the Washington Square campus had separate student newspapers until 1973 when the University Heights campus was closed. The Washington Square Journal was preceded by the Washington Square Bulletin and succeeded by the Washington Square Daily News.

Scope and Contents

This collection contains issues of student newspapers the Square Journal and Washington Square Journal dating from 1955-1973. The newspaper was printed daily and documented student life, university news, and local or international news with relevancy to NYU.
