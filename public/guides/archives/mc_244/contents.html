Series V: Photographs, 1924-2009

langmaterial

English

Scope and content

This series contains personal photographs, as well as images depicting Krishna Reddy's artistic career over time. Images in Series V include art prints of Reddy's drawings and photographic prints of his sculptures, as well as images of works by his artistic precursors and mentors, including Joan Miro, Ossip Zadkine, Rabindranath Tagore, and Heinz Leinfellner. Also depicted in the series are scenes from printmaking workshops and Reddy's 1972 receipt of the Padma Shri award. Many images of Reddy's own works contain detailed explanations of artistic technique on their reverse sides.
