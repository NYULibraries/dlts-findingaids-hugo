Abstract

C. William Britcher was a student at New York University's School of Public Administration in the early 1950s. This collection consists of a single item, Britcher's 1952 thesis for his Master of Public Administration titled "Quality Control of Small Lots."

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Donated by Mary Britcher, C. William Britcher's widow, in September 2017; the accession number associated with this gift is 2017.053.
