Scope and Contents

Material in this series largely contains material from Pauline Park's work with the New York Association for Gender Rights Advocacy (NYAGRA) and Queens Pride House from the 1900s to the mid 2010s. Most the materials in this series documents Park's work applying to various grants, speaking at various events,and writing letters regarding New York State's Dignity for All Students Act on behalf of NYAGRA. There are some material from other LGBT and Asian American organizations as well. Material in this series includes: board meeting minutes, audio recordings, notes, correspondence between Park and NYAGRA/QPH members, certificates of incorporation, grant proposals and reports, flyers, press, testimony, information packets, and brochures.

There is some born-digital media--one Word document--in this series.

Extent

Scope and Contents

This series contains published and unpublished creative, critical, academic, and personal written material by Park from the late 1990s to the mid 2010s. The material in this series includes drafts of personal essays, articles published in LGBT magazines, VHS tapes, published letters to newspaper editors, book chapters, poetry, a play script, and an audio recording of Park playing the piano.

There are some born-digital media--two audio files--in this series.

Scope and Contents

This series contains ephemera from the many events where Park was a speaker, panelist moderator, participant, or attendee. The bulk of the material from this series comes from 1997-2014. Of note in this series are flyers, programs, and press from 2005 when Park was the first openly transgender marshall in the New York City Pride March and name badges from events that Park participated in. There is a sound recording on two audiocassettes of an interview with Lee Chung-Woo (Chingusai-Seoul). Ephemera from this series include: flyers, programs, brochures, speaking contracts, speeches, digital images, photographs, testimony from court cases, correspondence, and legal notices from court cases.

There are some born-digital media--image files--in this series.
