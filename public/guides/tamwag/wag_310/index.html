Scope and Content Note

CWA Local 1060 represents workers at Lucent (formerly Bell Labs, in Murray Hill, NJ). This collection consists of material compiled by Raymond A. Singley (1920-2005), who served as a local steward and executive board member. The collection includes minutes, reports, stewards manuals, bargaining and grievance materials, and CWA memorabilia.
