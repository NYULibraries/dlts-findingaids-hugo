Scope and Contents

"The Good Fight," the 1983 feature-length documentary, pays homage to American volunteers who served with the International Brigades during the Spanish Civil War. The film makes extensive use of archival footage from the war, stills, primary documents, radio broadcasts, newsreels, and interviews. This collection consists of still photographs acquired during research for and production of the film.
