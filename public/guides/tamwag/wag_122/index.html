Scope and Contents

Norah Chase, an associate professor of English at Kingsborough Community College in Brooklyn, N.Y., and a long-time activist in the Professional Staff Congress (PSC), the labor union representing college teachers in the City University of New York system, donated these materials. This collection contains copies of Unity News, pamphlets, flyers, correspondence, and meeting minutes related to the 2000 election of PSC officers and representatives by Chase and other members of the union's Unity Caucus.
