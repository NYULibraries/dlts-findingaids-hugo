Scope and Contents

This very small collection contains some records of the American Federation of State, County, and Municipal Employees (AFSCME), DC 37, Local 1306, which represented workers in the American Museum of Natural History. The collection contains correspondence, collective bargaining documents, grievances, and minutes of the local.
