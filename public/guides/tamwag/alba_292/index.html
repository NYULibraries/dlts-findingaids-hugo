Scope and Contents

This collection consists mostly of correspondence between Abraham Lincoln Brigade volunteer Louis Cohen and his family during 1937 and the subsequent correspondence between Cohen's family and the U.S. Government, International Red Cross, and other organizations after Louis went missing in 1938.
