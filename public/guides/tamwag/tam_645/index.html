Abstract

The Union Square Awards was established in 1998 to provide support for grassroots activism throughout the five boroughs of New York City. This collection consists primarily of brochures and photographs from the Union Square Awards' ceremonies and events as well as photographs of organizations and their members who received financial support. The collection also contains annual reports for the organization throughout the years 1998-2011 as well as two special reports.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Historical/Biographical Note

The Union Square Awards was established in 1998 to provide support for grassroots activism throughout the five boroughs of New York City. 187 organizations have received the Union Square Award, which provides a general assistance grant of $50,000 to an emerging organization as well as technical assistance and the opportunity to apply for regrants. In 2006 the Union Square Arts Award was established to recognize organizations that provide educational opportunities in the arts to low-income communities. This award consists of a $35,000 grant, technical assistance and regrant opportunities. Beginning in 2004, the Union Square Awards has provided over 300 individual grants of $25,000 to eligible organizations that had received a Union Square Award in the past and required further assistance to meet their goals.

In 2012 the Union Square Fund Board announced that it will no longer be issuing new grants to applicants. The Union Square Fund will be scaling down all activities by December 2014 and will continue to provide technical assistance and support to active grantees throughout that period.

Scope and Contents

This collection consists primarily of brochures and photographs from the Union Square Awards' ceremonies and events as well as photographs of organizations and their members who received financial support. Many of the photographs are undeveloped negatives and almost all of the printed photographs are in black-and-white. There are various CDs containing additional images of the organization's events throughout its history as well as logo files for the Union Square Awards. The collection also contains annual reports for the organization throughout the years 1998-2011 as well as two special reports, a 10-year report and a Social Justice Artists' Collaborative Survey Report.

Processing Information

During accessioning, photographic materials that were in binders were taken out of the binders and put into folders. Plastic coverings of photographs were not removed. Images on CDs were taken out of binders and placed in CD covers within a compact disc box. A collection level finding aid was created to describe the materials.

In 2014, the archived website was added as a series.

Arrangement

The order in which these materials were sent to the Tamiment Library has been maintained.
