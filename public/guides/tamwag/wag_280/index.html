Abstract

Herman W. Benson is a union member and labor journalist. This collection contains a typescript of an unpublished paper by Herman W. Benson regarding the reform movement in the Brotherhood of Painters, Decorators and Paperhangers in the 1960s.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Materials found in repository; provenance is unknown. The accession number associated with this collection is 1950.280.

Historical/Biographical Note

Herman W. Benson was a member of the United Auto Workers and the United Rubber Workers. As a labor journalist, he served as the trade union editor of Labor Action and as editor and publisher of Union Democracy in Action. Benson is the author of Democratic Rights For Union Members: A Guide To Internal Union Democracy .

Scope and Contents
