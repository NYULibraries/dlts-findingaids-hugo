Abstract

Julius Schmulewitz (1895-1966) was a member of the Bakery and Confectionary Workers International Union, Local 3. This collection contains a small biographical manuscript on Julius and Lilly Schmulewitz, written by their son Jack Schmulowitz.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Donated by Jack Schmulowitz in 1999. The accession number 1999.020 is associated with this gift.

Historical/Biographical Note

Julius Schmulewitz (1895-1966) was a member of the Bakery and Confectionary Workers International Union, Local 3. Julius, and his wife Lilly Schmulewitz (1902-1968) immigrated from Poland during the 1920s. Their son, Jack Schmulowitz, wrote this biographical manuscript about their lives in 1999.

Scope and Contents

This collection contains a small manuscript on Julius and Lilly Schmulewitz. The manuscript covers the lives of Julius and Lilly, and Julius's work with the Bakery and Confectionary Workers International Union, Local 3. The manuscript also contains several maps and tables.
