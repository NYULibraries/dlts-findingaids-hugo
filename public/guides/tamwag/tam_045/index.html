Abstract

The Springfield Cooperative Society, founded in 1911, was a consumer cooperative for the purchase of clothing, coal, and groceries. Fragmentary records found here are constitutions, correspondence, financial records, minutes of board of directors meetings, and stock certificates.

Immediate Source of Acquisition
