Abstract

Thomas Moran was the Safety Director for the Utility Workers Union of America, Local 1-2. This collection contains materials related to Local 1-2.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Donated by Thomas Moran in 2000. The accession number associated with this gift is 2000.012.

Historical/Biographical Note

Thomas Moran was the Safety Director for the Utility Workers Union of America, Local 1-2, and in this position worked with the White Lung Association, the New York Committee for Occupational Safety and Health to advocate for the removal of asbestos all over New York City.
