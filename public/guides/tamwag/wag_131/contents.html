This series contains originals and photocopies of bylaws and proposed bylaws of Local 1-2, the Joint Council of Locals 1-2 and 4, and the Joint Council of the Brotherhood of Consolidated Edison Employees; a small amount of correspondence between Joseph A. Series IV: Election Records, 1940-2002

langmaterial

English

Extent

0.7 Linear Feet in 22 file folders and 2 oversize folders

Scope and Content Note

This series documents the contentious election history of the Local. Elections are well represented through leaflets, memos, clippings, ballot tallies, and official reports of arbitrators and court-appointed monitors. This series contains correspondence and memoranda between the locals, the companies, and the National Labor Relations Board regarding the planning of elections; notices of elections and election results; decisions of the National Labor Relations Board; notices with platforms and election slates for political parties within Local 1-2, including the Justice Party, the Unity Party, Members 1st Party, and Administration; lists of election observers; notices and posters for elections; polling lists; and election results. Correspondents include the Brotherhood of Consolidated Edison Employees; the Joint Council of Locals 1, 2, 3, 4, and 7; Consolidated Edison System Companies; Harold J. Straub; Elinore M. Herrick; Patrick McGrath; Augustus Low; Andrew McMahon; John C. Arnell; Edward L. Marsine; and Bernard E. Gallagher. Early elections are well represented, especially the 1940 election in which the Brotherhood of Consolidated Edison Employees became Local 1-2.

Subseries V.B: Contracts, 1935-2002, undated

langmaterial

English

Extent

1.5 Linear Feet in 1 record carton, 13 folders, 1 oversize folder, 1 VHS cassette, and 2 buttons

Scope and Content Note

This subseries contains drafts and printed agreements between Local 1-2 and ConEd; the Employees Representation Plans of Bronx Gas and Electric Company and Consolidated Edison Employees and the Consolidated Edison Company of New York; the Brotherhood of Consolidated Edison Employees and Consolidated Edison Company of New York; Joint Council of Locals 1, 2, 3, 4, and 7 and Consolidated Edison System Companies; and other locals and ConEd. Also included is a 1978 agreement between the Jamaica Water Supply Company and Local 1-2. The negotiations are documented through correspondence and memoranda between the locals, the companies, and the National Labor Relations Board regarding the creation of and deliberation around contracts and minutes of their negotiation meetings. Other items include payroll classifications and job titles booklets; lists of employees denied wage progression increases; issues of Bargaining Bulletin, The Record, and Utility Workers' Light; handbooks on mobilizing members; surveys of union members regarding where they work and what issues in the contract are important to them; notices to members regarding the contract process; newspaper clippings regarding contract negotiations; and buttons supporting ratification of different contracts. There are approximately 50 photographs of a rally held in Union Square, Manhattan in support of the 1992 contract.

Subseries V.E: Strikes, 1989-2002

langmaterial

English

Extent

0.4 Linear Feet in 12 folders, 1 D-2 cassette, and 1 button

Scope and Content Note

This subseries contains material from all stages of the strikes, from proposal to planning to strike to resolution. Most of the strikes were in response to failed contract negotiations between Local 1-2 and ConEd, but some of the material documents Local 1-2 striking in solidarity with other unions, including the Communications Workers of America in 1989 and the Utility Workers Union of America, Locals 375 and 381 in 1998. This material includes Local 1-2 Bargaining Bulletins, notices, and posters communicating the position of the Local. Material regarding the planning of strikes includes picket duty lists, schedules, and other planning documents, as well as correspondence between officials from the Local and the National, ConEd officials, and politicians. Other materials include newspaper clippings and VHS cassettes with news reports on the strikes; photographs of demonstrations and rallies; and buttons documenting political parties and positions in regards to the strikes. There are three photographs from a strike in the 1990s which could not be positively dated and are filed at the end of the material.

Materials added to the collection in 2017 include additional photos, flyers, and a scrapbook related to the 1998 Local 381 Strike. They also contain videos of press coverage of demonstrations demonstration against Con-Edison and in support of the Indian Point Utility Workers Union (UAUW, Local 1-2), and a small assortment of bargaining agreements and bylaws.

Series VI: General Files, 1927, 1941-2006

langmaterial

English

Extent

1.9 Linear Feet in 2 record cartons, 1 oversize folder, 3 VHS cassettes, 3 CDs, 2 audio cassettes, 1 audio reel, and 10 buttons

Scope and Content Note

This series contains correspondence, newspaper clippings, newsletters, photographs, programs, and VHS cassettes regarding a number of topics, including different political parties within the Local, including Administration, New Directions Caucus, Members 1st Party, and the Justice Party; the trusteeship instituted by the National in 1998 due to alleged mismanagement of funds by the president of the Local, Joseph Flaherty; histories of the Local, ConEd, and other organizations; the 50th anniversary of the local; social events held by the Local; efforts by the Progressive Labor Party to influence Local members; Local financial records; legislative files; the Local's dealings with individual employers other than ConEd (small numbers of Local members were employed by private companies); and the 1940s detail organizing efforts within ConEd which resulted in the replacement of older company unions by the Brotherhood of Consolidated Edison Employees.

Series VIII: Conferences and Conventions Files, 1948-2003

langmaterial

English

Extent

0.5 Linear Feet in 1 manuscript box, 1 oversize folder, 6 1/4" audio reels, 9 audio cassettes, and 16 CDs

Scope and Content Note

This series contains correspondence and notices regarding election of delegates to UWUA National Conventions; agenda and minutes of conference sessions at the UWUA Nuclear Conference; and audio recordings of Educational and Regional Conferences held in 1964.

UWUA Constitutional Conventions are documented through proceedings, officers reports, and photographs. The proceedings include transcripts of remarks and reports given at sessions. The officers reports outline important issues facing officers between conventions, including contract negotiations, financial operations of the National Union, and educational, political, and health initiatives. The photographs document panels, attendees, speakers, and social events at the conventions.

Arrangement

This series is arranged alphabetically by convention title and chronologically within that arrangement.
