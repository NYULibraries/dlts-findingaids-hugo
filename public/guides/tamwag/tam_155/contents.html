Scope and Content Note

NARAL Pro-Choice New York is a political and advocacy organization, whose mission is to guarantee every woman the right to make personal decisions regarding the full range of reproductive choices, including preventing unintended pregnancy, bearing healthy children, and choosing legal abortion. The website contains information on current and past projects, political endorsements, training manuals, reports, and news and event listings.
