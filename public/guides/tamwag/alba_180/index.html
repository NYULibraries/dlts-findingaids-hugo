Scope and Contents

This chronology was created by Jay Allen and Barbara Tuchman during the Spanish Civil War and covers events in Spain from Feb. 1936 through Nov. 1936. The single-spaced typed text densely covers oversized sheets that measure roughly 16" x 23". Each month has been separately foldered. There are also notes, drafts and fragments which have not been integrated into the larger chronology.
