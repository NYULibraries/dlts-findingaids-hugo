Abstract

This collection consists of materials documenting the functions and activities of the C.C. Gately Company, a Brooklyn contracting firm operated by Joseph T. Gately in the late 19th century. Materials include ledgers, cash books, and other financial records, including receipts and cancelled checks.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Donated by Dermot Gately in March of 2000. The accession number associated with this gift is 1997.030.

Historical/Biographical Note

The C.C. Gately Company (1856-1905) was a contracting firm operated by Joseph T. Gately in Brooklyn, New York.
