Interview is open for research, providing that the researcher follows the stipulation below, as agreed to in Schonfeld's release form for the interview:

"The tapes are available for approved research purposes with the proviso that any allegation of possible organized crime association with regard to any individual will not be made public in the lifetime of the individual, unless the allegation has been proven in a court of law."
