Board of Managers Brief Biographies and Photographs, Circa 1984

31142054610293box: 3 (Material Type: Mixed Materials)

Scope and Content Note

These files provide brief biographical information on members of the Board of Managers, including terms served, address, department, offices held, committees on which they participated, and a photograph. It is unclear when the organization began compiling this information, but it was maintained into the 1980s.

Photographs of World War I Veterans, undated

31142054610293box: 3 (Material Type: Mixed Materials)

Scope and Content Note

Compiled album of photographs of gas company employees who served in the First World War. Often includes brief biography or obituary for veterans killed during the war.
