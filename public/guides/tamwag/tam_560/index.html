Historical/Biographical Note

Richard Lee Winger (1943-), of San Francisco, CA, is the publisher and editor of Ballot Access News (1985- ), and is on the editorial board of the Election Law Journal. He publishes analysis, statistics and legal information and supports more equitable laws allowing access to the ballot for minor parties. In 1985 Winger helped found, along with several minor party representatives, including the Communist Party's Si Gerson, the Coalition for Free and Open Elections (COFOE). The group attempts to co-ordinate action and provide mutual support among the various minor parties for efforts to liberalize and reform ballot access laws.

Scope and Content Note

This small unprocessed collection contains correspondence and writings, internal documents and printed ephemera from progressive and radical political parties, spanning the years 1973-1991, including the Citizens Party, the Green Party, the National Unity Party, the Socialist Party, John Anderson's 1980 Presidential candidacy, and a file from the National Committee for Independent Political Action.
