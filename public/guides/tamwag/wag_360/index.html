Abstract

The Lynn Porter-Bernadotte Papers contain photographs, newspaper clippings, and legal documents related to Lynn Porter-Bernadotte's career in the New York City Fire Department. Porter-Bernadotte was one of the first women firefighters in New York City from 1982 to 2000.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Biographical Note

Lynn Porter-Bernadette was one of the first women firefighters in New York City. She served in the New York City Fire Department from 1982-2000.
