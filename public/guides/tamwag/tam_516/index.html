Abstract

This collection contains circulars, fliers, leaflets, newsletters, and other ephemera accumulated by Leonard Sklar, documenting a broad range of progressive/left politics in the 1980s.

Scope and Contents

Printed ephemera (circulars, fliers, leaflets, newsletters, etc.) accumulated by Leonard Sklar, documenting a broad range of progressive/left politics in the 1980s, including animal rights, economic and social justice, electoral politics (including the 1988 campaign of Jesse Jackson to become the Democratic Party Presidential candidate) environmentalism, peace movements and international solidarity (Central America, Iran), radical political parties, etc.
