Abstract

Time's Up is a New York City environmentalist organization whose principal activity is the promotion of bicycle riding and the rights of cyclists through its Critical Mass campaigns. This collection consists of photographs, ephemera, and clippings related to Time's Up environmental organizing in New York City. These documents include posters, pamphlets, zines, flyers, and events calendars created to promote Critical Mass demonstrations and other acts of protest, events and celebrations, and fundraisers for Time's Up. They also include several binders containing local articles related to bike safety, the construction of bike lanes, and cycling events.

Scope and Content Note

This collection consists of materials created and collected by Time's Up in the course of their environmental activism. Materials includes posters, pamphlets, zines, flyers, and event calendars created to promote the organization's events and fundraisers, with particular emphasis on Critical Mass demonstrations and other acts of protest. The collection also includes several binders containing local articles related to bike safety, the construction of bike lanes, and cycling events.
