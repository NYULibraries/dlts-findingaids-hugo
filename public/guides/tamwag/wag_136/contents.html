Series III: International Brotherhood of Teamsters Locals, 1961-1994,

langmaterial

English

Scope and Content Note

Series III is arranged by Local numbers; the material relate to specific campaigns undertaken in those locals. Some records relate to pension fund campaigns and the efforts of TDU staff, in particular Susan Jennik to secure pension benefits for members. Additional materials include election records, mediation and arbitration awards; NLRB cases and grievance files; and collective bargaining. The records of Local 560, with its history of corruption and lengthy government-imposed trusteeship are of particular interest.

Series VI: Legal, 1977-1994,

langmaterial

English

Scope and Content Note

Series VI is organized by court cases, many involving pension and benefit funds. The records also include materials from the government's case against the international union, U.S. v. La Cosa Nostra. Individuals featured include Dellacroce and Salerno. Some material relates to Jackie Presser's role as a government informant (see "Identity of Informant known as "Alpro.") The records contain the settlement and documents pertaining to supervision of the first direct election in the union's history. Materials developed by the TDU in preparation for the election are included in this series.
