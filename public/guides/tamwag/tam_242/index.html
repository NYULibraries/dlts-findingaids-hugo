Abstract

The collection consists of the one movie script by Mary Engelberg entitled The Winner. The movie begins in 1936 in Monessen, PA, and chronicles the rise of a corrupt union official.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Donated by Mary Engelberg in 1995. The accession number associated with this gift is 1995.021.

Historical/Biographical Note

Mary Engelberg, aka Mary Engel, was the author of a labor-themed movie script/screenplay.

Scope and Contents note

Consists of The Winner, a movie script/screenplay (114 pp.) It begins in 1936 in a union hall, in Monessen, PA (the local union represents workers at the "Spartacus Tin Plate Company"). It concludes years later with union corruption and murder, with the word "winner" in the title apparently referring to a corrupt union official who rose out of the rank and file.
