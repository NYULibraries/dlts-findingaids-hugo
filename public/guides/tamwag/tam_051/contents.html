Scope and Contents

The New American Movement June 2018 Accretion contain position paper publications from 1972 to 1981. Position paper topics address discrimination against women, racism, foreign policy, occupational health and safety, fair taxation, housing and other economic issues.

Arrangement

This series has not been arranged by an archivist. The materials are arranged in the order in which they were received from the donor.
