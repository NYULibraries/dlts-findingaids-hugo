Series I: Correspondence, 1937-1938

Scope and Content Note

The series consists mostly of letters sent by Harold Smith from Spain to a girlfriend, identified only as "Jeanette." Also included are one letter to his sister(?) Clara, one from a fellow volunteer, Harold "Jack" Boxer, ansd three undated fragments.
