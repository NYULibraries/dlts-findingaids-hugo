Abstract

Stephen C. Vladeck was the husband of Judith Vladeck and worked as a labor and civil rights lawyer and collective bargainer in higher education. This collection contains some of Vladeck's personal and professional correspondence from the 1960s to 1970s.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Donated by Judith Vladeck in 1992. The accession number associated with this gift is 1992.022.

Historical/Biographical Note

Stephen C. Vladeck was the husband of Judith Vladeck and worked as a labor and civil rights lawyer and collective bargainer in higher education. Vladeck was also counsel to and secretary-treasurer of the Peoples Educational Camp Society, an active member of the Tamiment Institute and Robert F. Wagner Labor Archives at New York University.
