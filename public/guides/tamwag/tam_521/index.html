Abstract

The Westgate Tenants Association represents the tenants of a "Mitchell-Lama" built housing project on New York City's Upper West Side, and in particular struggles to maintain rent stabilization against the project's new owner, who opted out of the Mitchell-Lama" program and seeks to achieve market-rate rents in the project. The collection contains correspondence, minutes, flyers, newsletters, clippings, research files, and government reports related to housing issues.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Donated by Jean Green Dorsey on behalf of the Westgate Tenants Association in 2009. The accession number associated with this collection is 2009.093.
