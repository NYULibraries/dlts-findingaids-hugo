Abstract

Morris Paris was a radio news commentator for WEVD, and assistant deputy controller of New York, N.Y. from 1938 to 1963. The papers contain typewritten manuscripts of "Review of the Week" (1943-1946), news commentary on WEVD, the Jewish Daily Forward Radio Station. Papers also include a series of articles titled, "Limited Dividend Housing becomes a Racket" (1934).
