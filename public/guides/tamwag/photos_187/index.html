Abstract

The Roger Keeran Photographs document a meeting and a picnic held by District Council 65 of the United Automobile Workers (UAW). The dates and locations of both events are unknown.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

The provenance of this collection is unknown. The accession numbers associated with this collection are 1950.039 and NPA.2001.002.

Historical/Biographical Note

Scholar Roger Keeran publicly identifies as a member of the Communist Party, and as of 2013, Keeran is the Labor and Policy Studies Coordinator at Empire State College in New York. Keeran has published widely on subjects related to labor, communism, and the Left.

District Council 65 began its affiliation with the UAW in 1979. In 1994, District Council 65 dissolved as a result of bankruptcy.
