Abstract

The Mimi Pichey Collection on Cuban Travel consists of color slides and travel ephemera accumulated by Mimi Pichey during a trip to Cuba in January 1980, as well as a speech, promotional event flier, and audio recording from Pichey's presentation at Cuba Today: An Eyewitness Report, an event organized by the Socialist Workers Party which took place on February 24, 1980 in Saint Paul, Minnesota. During the lecture, Pichey shared her travel experiences and findings regarding the political status of Cuba. This collection also contains an unrelated presentation script, a labor publication, meeting notes, and outgoing correspondence.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Biographical Note

Mimi Pichey was a member of the Socialist Workers Party. In January 1980, Pichey traveled to Cuba on a fact-finding trip. She recounted these experiences and shared slides at Cuba Today: An Eyewitness Report in Saint Paul, Minnesota on February 24, 1980.

Scope and Contents

The Mimi Pichey Collection on Cuban Travel consists of color slides Mimi Pichey captured during a trip to Cuba in January 1980, as well as a speech, promotional event flier, and audio recording of her presentation at Cuba Today: An Eyewitness Report on February 24, 1980 in Saint Paul, Minnesota. Organized by the Socialist Workers Party, Cuba Today: An Eyewitness Report featured Pichey sharing her experiences and findings about the political status of Cuba from her trip. The collection also includes travel ephemera, such as a map of Havana and a restaurant menu. Other materials include a script for a separate presentation, a labor publication, meeting notes, and outgoing correspondence.
