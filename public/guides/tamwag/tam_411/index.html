Abstract

Dan Georgakas is an anarchist,labor historian, scholar of Greek-American redicalism, and of motion pictures. Coauthor of Detroit: I Do Mind Dying and coeditor of the Encyclopedia of the American Left and he is a long time member of the Cineaste editorial board, and has taught part time at New York University and Queens College. The collection contains correspondence, manuscripts and subject files, most pertaining to Latin American cinema and his work for Cineaste.
