Historical/Biographical Note

Yale University graduate students / graduate teaching assistants attempted to organize labor unions in the 1980s-1990s. The two key organizations were the Graduate Employees and Student Organization, and the Graduate Student Union.

Scope and Contents

The collection includes GESO Voice: newsletter of the Graduate Employees and Student Organizations, notes from meetings, findings from a graduate student employee survey, newspaper clippings, research re: other graduate student attempts to organize, statements from the Graduate Student Union and officials from Yale University and a timeline of teaching assistant solidarity at Yale.
