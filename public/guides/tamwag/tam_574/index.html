Abstract

Kenneth P. Neilsen is an educator, artist, composer, publisher and author, and friend of Frederic Ewen. Ewen (1899-1989) was a professor of English from Brooklyn College who was forced to resign in 1952 because of his progressive politics and resistance to McCarthyism, and who later formed a theater company. The collection contains correspondence, notes, clippings, and playbills.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Donated by Kenneth P. Neilson in 2011. The accession number associated with this gift is 2011.030.

Historical/Biographical Note

Kenneth P. Neilsen is an educator, artist, composer publisher and author, and was a Friend of Frederic Ewen (1899-1989), a professor of English from Brooklyn College who was forced to resign in 1952 because of his progressive politics and resistance to McCarthyism, and who later formed a theater company that produced plays. Neilson co-operated with Ewen in several such endeavors.
