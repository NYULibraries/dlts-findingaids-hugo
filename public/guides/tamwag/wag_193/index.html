Abstract

The Federation of Shorthand Reporters consists of members who do stenographic reporting for law firms in New York City. The Federation of Shorthand Reporters Records date from 1968 to 1986 and contain contracts, correspondence, clippings, and legal filings that document the union's strikes, resolved grievances, arbitration, association with District Council 1707, and general membership.

Scope and Contents

The Federation of Shorthand Reporters Records date from 1968 to 1986 and contain contracts, correspondence, clippings, and legal filings that document the union's strikes, resolved grievances, association with District Council 1707, and general membership. Strikes in 1968, 1980, and 1986 are well documented with clippings, correspondence, and internal notes. A large portion of the collection consists of files from legal counsel representing the union in arbitrations and an anti-trust case. Files on the union's Ad Hoc Computer Committee are also included.
