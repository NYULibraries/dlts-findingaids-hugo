Abstract

The International Alliance of Theatrical Stage Employes, Moving Picture Technicians, Artists and Allied Crafts of the United States and Canada (better known as IATSE) is a union of workers in the entertainment industry. Originally composed of stage hands and projectionists, IATSE now represents craftspeople in a broad array of related occupations. This collection consists of IATSE contracts submitted to the Secretary Treasurer of IATSE. The contracts are organized by local number.
