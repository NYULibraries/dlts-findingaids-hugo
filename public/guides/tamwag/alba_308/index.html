Abstract

This collection of Spanish Civil War Letters documents events and activities that occurred prior to and post war from 1929 to 1944. The collection is comprised of correspondence in both letter and postcard formats. The handwritten letters are from different participants and times during the war, and include written communication between soldiers and their family members; government officials; prisoners of war; and others. This collection documents particular activities of the Spanish Civil War from the personal perspectives of these individuals.

Processing Information

At the time of accessioning, materials were removed from their plastic bags and placed in new acid-free folders and boxes. Materials that were loose was foldered and titled. Original caption information, when available, was transcribed onto acid-free archival folders. Where captions were illegible, the archivist's interpretation is enclosed in square brackets. All materials were organized in the order the content was received. A letter and an envelope from Folder 4 was treated for mold. These materials were returned to its original folder.

Arrangement

This collection has not been arranged by an archivist. The materials are arranged in the order in which they were received from the donor.
