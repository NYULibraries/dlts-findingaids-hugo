Series II: Office records, 1979-2002

Extent

2.75 Linear Feet
75.24 Megabytes in 69 electronic files

Scope and Contents

Series II contains administrative records and background materials from the Harry Van Arsdale Labor History Project. Included are funding and grant application records, correspondence with staff and student workers administering the project, project forms and mailing lists, clippings and publicity materials, and syllabi from related courses taught by Janet Greene.
