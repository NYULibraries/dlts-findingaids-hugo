Immediate Source of Acquisition

On Davis sent a gift of his papers on squatters' rights in 2006. The accession number associated with this gift is 2006.021.

Historical/Biographical Note

On Davis was involved in the 1980s squatters' rights movement in New York City's East Village which culminated in the Tompkins Square Park Police Riot of August 1988.

Scope and Contents

The collection consists of subject files, clippings, oversized artwork, account books and a diary composed by squatters at 537 East 13th Street.
