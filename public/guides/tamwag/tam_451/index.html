Historical/Biographical Note

Walter Scott Neff was a psychologist and author of several books, including Work and Human Behavior, and was also head of the American Peace Mobilization, a Communist-associated organization, founded in 1940.

Scope and Contents

This collection contains FBI files obtained through a FOIA (Freedom of Information Act) request. The files cover FBI observations of Walter Neff and his activities with Communist-affiliated groups and organizations.
