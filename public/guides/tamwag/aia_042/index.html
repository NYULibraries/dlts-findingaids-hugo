Abstract

The materials in this collection were collected and assembled by the staff of the Archives of Irish America and various donors in an attempt to document ethnic and cultural aspects of the 1952 John Ford film, The Quiet Man. The collection includes an original 35mm print and an oversized theater poster of the film.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

The provenance of the materials in this collection is unknown. The accession number associated with the materials is 1997.033.

Scope and Contents

This collection consists of memorabilia related to John Ford's 1952 film The Quiet Man. The collection includes an original 35mm print, an oversized theater poster of the film, and movie posters.
