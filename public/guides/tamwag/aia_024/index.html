Abstract

The collection, collected by John McLaughlin, consists of articles, newspaper clippings and posters related to the Irish Hunger Strikes and other Irish Republicanism activities. Both the American and Irish press are represented.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Materials were collected by John McLaughlin and donated to the Archives of Irish America in 2000. The accession number associated with this collection is 1997.014.

Historical/Biographical Note

The materials in this collection were collected by Irish American political activist John McLaughlin.

Scope and Contents

The collection contains ephemeral material related to and documenting reaction to the Hunger Strikes of 1981 and and to the organization Irish Northern Aid. Contents include newspaper clippings and magazine articles, and posters relating to the Hunger Strikers at Long Kesh Prison.
