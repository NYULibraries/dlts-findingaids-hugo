Series I: Samuel Hall and Robert George Thompson Materials

Scope and Content Note

Series I contains a small portion of material related to Sylvia's first husband Samuel Hall such as a marriage certificate, photographs, Sylvia Hall Thompson's reminisces of her husband, and clippings related to Hall's activism and his death in 1954. Material related to Robert Thompson includes a marriage certificate, correspondence, and photographs.This series also contains several articles by Robert Thompson, which were published in Political Affairs and materials related to Sylvia Hall Thompson's involvement with the Veterans of the Abraham Lincoln Brigade.

Series II: Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) FBI FIles on Sylvia Thompson

Scope and Content Note

Series II primarily consists of Freedom of Information Act release files related to Federal Bureau of Investigation surveillance of Hall throughout the 1950s and 1960s. Thompson requested copies of these files in the 1980s. Correspondence between Thompson and various government agencies is also included. Additionally, this series contains one cassette tape titled "Nuchow interview" and an encased coin commemorating the 50th anniversary of the Antifascist Spanish War.

Series III: Thompson v. McNamara Trial Materials

Scope and Content Note

Series III "Thompson v. McNamara" contains materials related to Sylvia Thompson's efforts to have her late husband interred in Arlington National Cemetery. It includes court documents exhibits and briefs, press clippings, photographs and correspondence. Also included is "The Arlington Case: Robert Thompson-story of an unburied soldier," which is a pamphlet published by Sylvia Thompson in 1967 in an effort to publicize her attempts at interring her husband in Arlington National Cemetery.
