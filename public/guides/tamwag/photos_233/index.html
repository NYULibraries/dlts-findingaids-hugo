Abstract

Robert Minor (1884-1952) was a cartoonist, journalist, and prominent member of the Communist Party of the United States of America. The collection contains two photographs. One photograph depicts Antonio Lombardo, and the other appears to be Robert Minor.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

The provenance of this collection is unknown. The accession number associated with this collection is 2006.016.

Scope and Content Note

The collection contains two photographs. One photograph is signed, "To Comrade 'Bob' Minor, for whom I have the highest revolutionary regard. From, Antonio Lombardo." The other photograph is not labeled, but appears to be Minor himself.
