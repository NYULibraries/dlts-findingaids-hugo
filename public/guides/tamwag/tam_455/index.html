Historical/Biographical Note

The Socialist News was formed by the merger of the Cleveland Socialist and Workers' Cause. It was published by first by the Local Cleveland, Socialist Party, beginning in 1914, then by the Local Cuyahoga County, Socialist Party, ending in 1919. The Cleveland Plain Dealer, founded in 1845, is the largest daily newspaper in Ohio.

Scope and Contents

This small collection contains photocopied material from a scrapbook containing clippings on local and national radical activity. The majority of the clippings come from the Cleveland Plain Dealer and the Socialist News of Cleveland with a few clippings from other papers local to the Cleveland, Ohio area. The clippings span from 1915-1919 with a few items from the mid-1920s. The original scrapbook, of unknown provenance, was in fragile and fading condition and was disbound and recopied.
