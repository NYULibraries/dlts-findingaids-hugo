Historical/Biographical Note

The International Alliance of Theatrical and Stage Employees, Local 52 was founded in the early 1920s, and officially chartered by the International Alliance of Theatrical Stage Employees (IATSE) in 1924.

Scope and Contents

IATSE, Local 52 (Motion Picture and Sound Mechanics) records include membership directory, Local 52 newsletters, 50th anniversary program, Executive Board minutes (1966-1990), budget meeting minutes, general membership meeting minutes, and by-laws.
