Historical/Biographical Note

Randall B. "Pete" Smith (1917-1989) served in Abraham Lincoln Brigade during Spanish Civil War, and later moved to New York where he was a radical activist within the National Maritime Union in the 1930s and 1940s. Smith subsequently worked as marketing executive.
