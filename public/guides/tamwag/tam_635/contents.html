Series I: Brecht Forum Records, 1960s-2014

Scope and Contents

This series contains records of Brecht Forum (dated 1960s-2014). It includes organizational correspondence, public programming files, course catalogues, fundraising files, meeting minutes, financial records, and newsletters. The series also contains numerous audio recording of conferences, public events, and lectures.

Scope and Content Note

The Brecht Forum, originally located at 451 West Street in Manhattan, now at 388 Atlantic Avenue in Brooklyn, is a Marxist cultural and educational center. The Brecht Forum was founded in 1975 as The New York Marxist School. In 1984, the founding collective incorporated as The Brecht Forum with The New York Marxist School as the Forum's core project. In addition to the New York Marxist School, it also offers other programs, language classes, and arts and cultural events. The website contains information on their curriculum and classes, as well as economic news analysis.

Series II: Mary Boger New York Marxist School Records, 1970s-2000s

Scope and Content Note

The series contains early New York Marxist School records maintained by founding member Mary Boger. It includes NYMS documents; minutes, reports, curricula, catalogs, course syllabi and reading materials, programs, newsletters, public events documents, Marxist Education Collective documents, including conference materials; materials about the Grenada revolution and its overthrow, including tapes and slides; Science Task Force materials; Philadelphia Marxist School materials; topical files (political economy, Russian Revolution, etc.); files on several countries, including Cuba, El Salvador, Portugal, Puerto Rico, etc.; Women's movement materials; cultural magazines; and files on the World Trade Center Trial.
