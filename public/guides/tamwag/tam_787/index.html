Abstract

Jules P. Kirsch is a New York-based attorney. He was a member of the Lawyers' Constitutional Defense Committee in the 1960s, focusing on civil rights efforts. This collection consists of materials created and collected by Kirsch concerning cartoonist Art Young, including correspondence between Kirsch and photographer Berenice Abbot and a photographic print of Young.
