Series V: Scrapbooks, 1939-1948

Scope and Content Note

This series contains materials from disassembled scrapbooks. It includes clippings, election materials related to Cacchione's run for councilman, holiday cards, correspondence, event invitations and programs, flyers related to Communist Party campaigns and events, and articles by and about Cacchione. The series also contains an oversized Scrapbook which includes clippings, flyers, press releases, and a small number of photographs relating to Cacchione's political career.
