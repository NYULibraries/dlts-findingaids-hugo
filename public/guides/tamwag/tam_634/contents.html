Series II: Videocassettes, 1984-1987

Scope and Content Note

Videos in this series include 21 recordings of Soh Diako performances, dated 1984-2000. Many of these tapes were recorded on Sony DVCAM and include videos of footage collected as part of the Soh Daiko Archival Project. These tapes each feature one to three videos of different performances at workshops, festivals, and other cultural events. Other events are recorded on Umatic and VHS tapes and include footage of individual performances at cultural instutions like the American Museum of Natural History and Japan Society. Several of these videos may be duplicated on the Soh Daiko Archival Project tapes.

Series III: Archived Websites, August 2014-ongoing

Extent

2 websites in two archived websites.

Scope and Contents

Two websites related to Soh Daiko, a Taiko drumming group. The websites contain event listings, videos of their performances, images, information about recruitment and their 40th anniversary concert, and how to financially support the group.

Accruals

New site crawls are accrued semiannually.

Appraisal

Crawl was limited to domains and subdomains of sohdaiko.org in order to remain within the collection scope and data constraints.

Physical Characteristics and Technical Requirements

Due to technical or privacy issues, archived websites may not be exact copies of the original website at the time of the web crawl. Certain file types will not be captured dependent on how they are embedded in the site. Other parts of websites that the crawler has difficulty capturing includes Javascript, streaming content, database-driven content, and highly interactive content. Full-Text searches of archived websites are available at https://archive-it.org/organizations/567.
