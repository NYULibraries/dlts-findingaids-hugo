Historical/Biographical Note

Robert F. Eckersdorff was president of the IBPAT, Local 490 (Paperhangers).

Scope and Contents

This collection contains papers documenting his role as president of the union and the history of the union itself. Documents include correspondence with congressmen, The Paperhanger (newsletter of Local 490), a history of the paperhangers union, newspaper clippings, election leaflets, writings of Eckersdorff for the union, and photographs.
