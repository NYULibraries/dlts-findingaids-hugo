langmaterial

Scope and Contents

Two films related to the Spanish Civil War, one is a copy of Henri Cartier-Bresson's The Abraham Lincoln Brigade in Spain, and the other a Spanish-language film called Otro Crimen de Franco made by Cuban filmmakers.

Physical Characteristics and Technical Requirements

Digibeta, 37 min., Black and White, Silent and Sound

Note: A film print of Henri Cartier-Bresson's The Abraham Lincoln Brigade in Spain has been preserved. Preservation elements and digital derivatives are available.
