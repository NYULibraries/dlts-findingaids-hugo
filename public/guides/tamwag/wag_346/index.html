Abstract

The Collective Bargaining Agreements (1946-1993) contains documentation from major labor unions within the United States. The materials available in this collection consist of by-laws, summaries of member benefits, and annual program reports from media, entertainment, hospitality, fashion, graphic arts, and local state labor councils within the United States. A significant portion of the collection consists of agreements made between the Newspaper Guild of New York, Local No. 3, of the American Newspaper Guild and other New York State publishers. The collection also features agreements established between the American Newspaper Guild and the International News Service, Department of The Hearst Corporation and the International News Photos. The collection also includes agreements established between the American Federation of Labor and Congress of Industrial Organizations (AFL-CIO) and other affiliated unions.
