Immediate Source of Acquisition

Donated by Conrad Firestein sent, 2005. The accession numbers associated with this gift are 2005.042 and NPA.2005.008.

Historical/Biographical Note

Conrad Firestein is an Amsterdam-based photographer whose portfolio documents fashion, beauty, lifestyles, and interiors from the late-20th century through the present. The subject of Firestein's collection, the late John Albok, was a celebrated tailor and photographer best known for his images of New York shot during the Great Depression.
