Series I: Records, 1905-1962

Scope and Content Note

This series contains the records of the Paperhangers union. The majority of this series is made up of ledgers, minute books, and financial records of the union, dating from 1800s to the middle of the century. Other types of material in the series include materials related to their Annual Eastern Seaboard Conference, flyers, correspondence, election materials, membership materials, and other ephemera. The series documents not only the day to day maintenance of the union, but also documents the union's support of other causes, including raising money for the State of Israel, and protesting in solidarity with other unions.

Series II: Photographs, 1937-1970s

Scope and Content Note

This series contains mostly black-and-white photographs, with some color photographs and negatives, depicting the Paperhangers union. Many deal with their annual Eastern Seaboard Conference. A portion of the black-and-white photographs were originally bound by a photo album. The photo album was photocopied and disassembled for preservation purposes. Other photographs depict portraits and other images of union members and protest photographs.
