Abstract

The Cooperative League of New York is a chapter of the reformist socialist organization,The Cooperative League of America, established by James Peter Warbasse in 1916.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Verna Rudd Kenvin sent a gift of glass negatives of the Cooperative League of New York in 1986. The accession numbers associated with this gift are 1950.151 and NPA.2001.103.

Historical/Biographical Note

The Cooperative League of New York Photographs document The Cooperative League of New York's hat store.
