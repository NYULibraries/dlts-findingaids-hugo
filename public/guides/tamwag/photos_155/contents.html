Conditions Governing Access

Audiovisual materials have not been preserved and may not be available to researchers. Materials not yet digitized will need to have access copies made before they can be used. To request an access copy, or if you are unsure if an item has been digitized, please contact Tamiment Library with the collection name, collection number, and a description of the item(s) requested. A staff member will respond to you with further information.
