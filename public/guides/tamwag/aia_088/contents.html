Series I. Education

Dates:
1977-2019

Extent

3.75 linear feet in 5 manuscript boxes and 1 oversize flat box

Scope and Contents

This series consists of materials documenting the Garveys teaching, and include organizational newsletters, conference programs, and agendas, meeting notes, and correspondence from various committee meetings. The activities of the American Irish Teachers Association and the Irish American Heritage and Culture Week Committee (a New York City Board of Education initiative) are particularly well represented. These materials focus almost exclusively on organizations and education initiatives devoted to increasing and improving the teaching Irish and Irish American culture and history, such as a proposed curriculum on the Irish Famine/Great Famine.

Dates:

Extent

Scope and Contents

The series documents the Garveys active engagement with various County Mayo-centered social organizations through photographs, newspaper articles, and programs for conventions and other annual events. Materials documenting the activities of the Mayo Society of New York comprise the majority of this series.

Series III. Irish American Organizations

Dates:
1935-2019

Extent

4 linear feet in 6 manuscript boxes and 1 oversize flat box

Scope and Contents

The series documents the Garveys engagement with Irish American organizations through photographs, newspaper articles, and programs for conventions and other annual events. The majority of the organizations in this series are in the New York City. The Ancient Order of Hibernians, the United Irish Counties Association of New York, and planning groups for various St. Patrick's Day parades are particularly well represented.
