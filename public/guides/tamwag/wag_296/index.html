Scope and Contents

This handwritten ledger (1889-1892) belonging to an unidentified tramcar company (whose President is noted as Mr. Thompson) provides information about tramcar workers employed by an unidentified company in New York City including names, rates of pay, and reasons for discharge. Some discharges are associated with a strike on January 29, 1889.
