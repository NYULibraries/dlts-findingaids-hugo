Scope and Content Note

Series II: Politics & Protests, 1974-1989

Dates:
1974-1989

Scope and Content Note

The photos in these series consist mainly of group shots at protests and picket lines in New York City. The majority of the people in the photos are not identified, although occassionaly there will be a shot of a more well-known speaker at a rally, like Paul O'Dwyer or Bernadette Devlin.
