Abstract

Morris Rosenzweig was the comptroller of District 65/United Automobile Workers. This collection contains clippings, booklets, buttons, photographs, artifacts, ephemera and a typescript on the history of District 65 written by Morris Rosenzweig.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Donated by Sheldon Rosenzweig (son) in 2008 and 2015. The accession numbers associated with this gift are 2008.004 and 2015.013.
