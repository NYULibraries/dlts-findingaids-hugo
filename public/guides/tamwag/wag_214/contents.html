Photographs: Copy Negatives of Prints of the Construction of the Singer Sewing Machine Building at Broadway and Liberty Street, New York CIty, circa 1907

31142062722569box: A005, folder: 2 (Material Type: negatives)

Scope and Content Note

These copy negatives were shot from a book, newspaper, or other printed material that was probably published not long after completion of construction of the Singer Sewing Machine Building (1908). All or most of the photographic prints located in Folder 1 in Box A005 of the records of the International Association of Bridge, Structural and Ornamental Iron Workers, Local 580 (WAG 214) were printed from this group of negatives.
