Abstract

1 box (.2 linear foot). Records primarily were gathered by Richard McAllan, former Local 2507 President, and senior NYC Paramedic. Documents also concern the New York City Health and Hospitals Corporation, and the EMTs and Paramedics of Local 2507 of New York, New York.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Marianne Pizzitola, President of the Uniformed FDNY EMS Retirees Association, Inc. sent a gift of Richard McAllan's files from his time as president of the Uniformed FDNY EMS Retirees Association in 2007. The accession number associated with this gift is 2009.033.
