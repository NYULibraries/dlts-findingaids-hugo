Photographs, circa 1913-1989

Scope and Content Note

112 photographic prints, all are black and white, with the exception of three color prints. Three are also oversized. Folder of materials on testimonial dinner dance held in honor of Joseph Magliacan includes original front cover for album that contained this group of photographs and booklet distributed at the event that contains a photograph, brief biographical sketch and greetings from Magliacano's friends, family, and supporters.
