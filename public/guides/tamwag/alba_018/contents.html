Scope and Content Note

This series is made up of full transcriptions of 65 of the oral histories that were conducted for Gerassi's project. Included here are transcripts for the following individuals: Bill Bailey, Alvah Bessie, Robert Colodny, Archie Brown, Walter Dent, Milt Felsen, Moe Fishman, John Gates, Carl Geiser, Robert Gladnick, Irving Goff, Syd Harris, Oscar Hunter, Evelyn Hutchins, Salaria Kee (a.k.a. Gabby Klein), Walter Scheutrum, Morris Stamm, Robert Steck, George Watt, Irving Weissman, and Milton Wolff.

Series II: Subject Files

langmaterial

English

Scope and Content Note

This series consists of clippings, articles, biographical files, research photocopies of correspondence from the Spanish Civil War-era, and some correspondence between Gerassi and individual veterans. Gerassi used these materials in the course of researching and writing his book. A file related to the Rockefeller Foundation grant received by Gerassi to fund this project can also be found here.

Series III: Unprocessed Materials, 1940s-2000s

Scope and Content Note

The unprocessed materials consist of federal government documents on John Gerassi and his family obtained through the Freedom of Information Act in the 1970s; subject files on various political topics, mainly from the 1970s; Gerassi's research and publicity materials for his biographies of Jean-Paul Sartre; Gerassi's articles and unpublished writings from the late 1960s-late 1980s; biographical materials; and loose news articles collected in the 2000s on a variety of political topics (some with annotations). There are also two reels of microfilm from the Hoover Institution labeled "Nuestra Industria Revista Economica" and approximately 50 cassette tapes, some on Sartre and some labeled "personal" or "political."
