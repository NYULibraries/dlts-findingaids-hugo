Abstract

Ivan Greenberg is an historian and author.This collecion contains FBI files obtained under the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) by Greenberg on the Socialist Scholars Conference, the Socialist Workers Party, Dissent magazine, the Institute for Policy Studies, and Gus Hall.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Donated by Ivan Greenberg in 2011. The accession number associated with this gift is 2011.025.

Historical/Biographical Note

Ivan Greenberg is an historian and the author of The Dangers of Dissent: The FBI and Civil Liberties Since 1965 (Lexington Books, 2010).
