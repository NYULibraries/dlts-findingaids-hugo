Abstract

Communications Workers of America, Local 1103 represents telecommunication workers in Westerchester and Putnam counties. The collection contains materials from the 1989 campaign and the archived website.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Donated by Robert McCracken, President of the Communications Workers of America, Local 1103, in 1994. The accession number associated with this gift is 1987.013.

http://www.cwa1103.org/ was initially selected by curators and captured through the use of The California Digital Library's Web Archiving Service in 2009 as part of Labor Unions and Organizations (U.S.) Web Archive. In 2015, the website was migrated to Archive-It. Archive-It uses web crawling technology to capture websites at a scheduled time and displays only an archived copy, from the resulting WARC file, of the website. In 2019, http://www.cwa1103.org/index.cfm?zone=/unionactive/past_articles.cfm&localnum=1103 was added to better capture the website. The accession number associated with this website is 2019.140.

Historical/Biographical Note

Communications Workers of America, Local 1103 received its charter on May 12, 1961 with 900 members. By the 1990s, the union had grown to representing over 2000 New York telephone workers in Westchester and Putnam counties. Daniel Keenan was president from 1964-1979 and Robert McCracken was president from 1989-2007. As of this writing (2014) Kevin Sheil is president of the union.

Scope and Content Note

The collection contains materials related to the 1989 union elections and lawsuits related to the union. It also includes the archived website for the union, which has been captured by NYU since 2009.
