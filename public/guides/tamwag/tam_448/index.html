Abstract

Vicki Morris was a reporter and editorilist for the Daily World as well as the daughter of George Morris, the Communist Party labor secretary. This small collection contains many clippings of articles and editorials of Morris's work as well as photographs of Morris.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Donated by Helen and George Morris in circa 1996. The accession number associated with this gift is 1997.070.

Historical/Biographical Note

Vicki Morris was a reporter and editorialist for the Communist Party, USA newspaper the Daily World (established 1968) and its successor, the People's Daily World, and the daughter of George Morris (1903-), an author, labor journalist, and the chair of the Labor Commission of the Communist Party, USA.
