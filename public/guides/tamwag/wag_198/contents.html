Series I: Organizational Records, 1976-2016

Scope and Contents

Series I consists of executive board meeting agendas, minutes, member lists, reports, and mailings; dues, audits, budgets, invoices, and other financial documents; board president documents and correspondence; annual conference documents and news releases; and the organization constitution and by-laws.

Series II: Special Events, Projects, and Publications, 1976-2016

Extent

5.5 Linear Feet in 4.5 record cartons, 2 manuscript boxes, and 3 shared boxes.
1 websites
1 videocassettes (vhs)
2 3.5" floppy disks

Scope and Contents

This series consists of documents, photographs, posters and broadsides pertaining to special events and projects, annual conferences, and organizational publications, such as Labor History Month, the John Commerford Award, the Barbara Wertheimer prize, film festivals, labor history tours, World History News, map projects, and occasional papers written by history students. This series also contains the organization's archived website and some unprocessed electronic records on physical carriers.

Due to technical or privacy issues, archived websites may not be exact copies of the original website at the time of the web crawl. Certain file types will not be captured dependent on how they are embedded in the site. Other parts of websites that the crawler has difficulty capturing includes Javascript, streaming content, database-driven content, and highly interactive content.
