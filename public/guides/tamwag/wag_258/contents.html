Series III: Photographs

Dates:
1981-1995, undated

Scope and Content Note

Series consists of 16 black-and-white and color glossy photographs, mostly 8x10, including group portraits from conferences and other gatherings in the early-1980s and 1995, and black-and-white documentary photographs of CWA workers on strike, including portraits of workers with strike signs that read, "CWA on Strike Against The Bell System," and photographs of union meetings.
