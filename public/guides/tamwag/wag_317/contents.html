Scope and Contents

This series consists of materials created and collected by LaTour in the course of researching, writing, and discussing her book Sisters in the Brotherhoods. Materials include correspondence with interview subjects, research files, oral history interview transcripts, and manuscripts drafts focused on women in the labor movement, focusing on blue-collar and trade employment. Topics that recur throughout these materials include discimination, welfare, vocational education, and pregnancy as it pertains to working women.
