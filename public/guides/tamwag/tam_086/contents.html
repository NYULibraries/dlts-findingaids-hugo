Series I: National Office, 1935-1980

Scope and Content Note

This series contains materials generated by the main office of the American Committee for Protection of Foreign Born from 1935-1980. The types of materials contained in this series include leaflets, articles, writings, correspondence, convention materials, circulars, press releases, and other ephemeral materials. Topics in the files include Nazi Germany, the Walter-McCarran Immigration Bill, Subversive Activities Control Board, deportation trials, and Haitian refugees.

Series II: Local Chapters, 1951-1957

Scope and Content Note

This series contains materials generated by local chapters in Los Angeles, Michigan, Minnesota, and the Midwest in the 1950s. These materials pertain to actions in the regions that are represented, for instance, the majority of materials in the Los Angeles chapter relate to Mexican Americans. The material is made up of press releases, leaflets, writings, and other materials.

Series III: Photographs, 1965

31142062761526box: 1, folder: 13 (Material Type: Mixed Materials)

Scope and Content Note

This series contains five black-and-white photographs of Ira Gollobin and Casimir Nowacki at the Polonia Club at 2nd Avenue and East 12th Street, New York, in support of Joseph Sherman's appeal on his case to the Supreme Court. In addition to the photographs, there is a clipping and letter by Gollobin on the event. Captions on the back of the photographs are supplied by Gollobin.
