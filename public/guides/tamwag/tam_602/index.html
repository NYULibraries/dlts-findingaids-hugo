Abstract

Joesph Elkind (1889-1974) was a member of the International Brotherhood of Painters and Allied Trades (IBPAT), specifically its New York City District Council 9. The collection contains membership cards, dues books, insurance policy materials and correspondence regarding insurance held through the IBPAT, and through the Workmen's Circle.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Materials found in collection; provenance is unknown. The accession number associated with this collection is 2011.144.

Historical/Biographical Note

Joesph Elkind (1889-1974) was a member of the International Brotherhood of Painters and Allied Trades (IBPAT), specifically its New York City District Council 9. (The IBPAT was known as the Brotherhood of Painters, Decorators and Paperhangers of America during most of the period of Elkind's membership.)
