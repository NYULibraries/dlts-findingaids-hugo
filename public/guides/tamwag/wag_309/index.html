Historical/Biographical Note

Carlton W. Werkau served as the Secretary-Treasurer for the Communications Workers of America (CWA) from 1943 until his death in 1955.

Scope and Contents

This collection consists of photographs, both personal and union related, correspondence, and CWA records. The CWA records consist of Convention proceedings from 1953-1955 as well as a strike manual and tax files. Articles and letters concerning Werkau's death in 1955 are also included.

Custodial History

A relative of Carlton W. Werkau sent these papers to the National Labor Relations Board (NLRB) Regional Office in Winston-Salem, NC. The collection was later transferred from the NLRB to The Communications Workers of America (CWA), where Werkau had served as Secretary-Treasurer from 1943-1955.
