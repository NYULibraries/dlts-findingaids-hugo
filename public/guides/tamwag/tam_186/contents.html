Writings, published and unpublished, and related correspondence. Most appear to be drafts, whole or partial, of his published books Atmospheric Destruction and Human Survival (1992), Marxism: A Living Science (1993), and Dialectical Materialism and Modern Science (1995). There is also a partial typescript for an unpublished book about Enver Hoxha; a folder titled "Factions" relating to the 1989-1991 internal struggle within the Communist Party USA; a folder containing several issues [1987-9] of, and correspondence relating to Red Academy, a publication of the W. Alphaeus Hunton College Teachers Club of the CPUSA. [The original issues have been separated for cataloging, with copies retained in the folder.], undated, 1980s-1990s
