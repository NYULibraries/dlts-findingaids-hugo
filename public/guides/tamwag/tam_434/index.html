Scope and Contents

This small collection includes writings on Camp Wo-Chi-Ca. Some documents also touch on Higley Hill, another camp run and staffed by the Communist Party of America. A set of photographs of Higley Hill and its campers from the early 1950's remains in the collection. The collection also contains a file of FBI and WPA papers on Herbert Gurewitz obtained through a Freedom of Information Act request.
