Abstract

The Harvard Graduate Students and Teaching Fellows Union was formed by 156 teaching fellows on March 13, 1972 in response to a change in the financial aid programs available to graduate students. The Union remained in existence through the 1972-1973 school year. This collection contains correspondence, membership lists, union statements, leaflets, newspaper clippings, posters and song lyrics.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Donated by Carol S. Lasser, 1978. The accession number associated with this gift is 1978.017.

Historical Note

The Harvard Graduate Students and Teaching Fellows Union was formed by 156 teaching fellows on March 13, 1972 in response to a change in the financial aid programs available to graduate students. The Union remained in existence through the 1972-1973 school year but no agreement was ever reached between the Union and the University.

Scope and Content Note

This collection contains correspondence, membership lists, union statements, leaflets, newspaper clippings, posters and song lyrics. There is also material from teaching assistant unions at other campuses. The collection covers the years 1967-1974, with the bulk of the material covering union activity from 1972-1973.
