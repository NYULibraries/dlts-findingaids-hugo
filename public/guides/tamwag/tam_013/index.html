Scope and Contents

This is a collection of 25 signed letters, all but four in French, by European anarchists, including: Fernand Brouez, Gabriel De La Salle, Henry Dupont, Sebastien Faure, Jean Grave, Pierre Kropotkin, Enrico Malatesta, Charles Malato, Louise Michel, David Nicole, Emile Pouget, Felix Pyat, Elisee Reclus, Henry Seymour, Wilhelm Spohr, Laurent Tailhade, and S. Yanovsky.
