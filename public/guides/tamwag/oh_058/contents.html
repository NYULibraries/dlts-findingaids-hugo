Series I: Democratic Socialist Organizing Committee, 1980

langmaterial

English

Scope and Contents

Materials in this series related to the "Eurosocialism and America" conference include a copy of the conference program and recordings of conference panels and plenary speeches. Not all panels from the conference were recorded. The printed conference schedule contains a full list of panels and speakers, with annotations indicating which sessions are included in the recordings. Notable individuals featured in the recordings include world leaders like Willy Brandt (Germany), Olof Palme (Sweden), Joop den Uyl (Netherlands), Francois Mitterand (France), as well as Michael Harrington, union leader William Winpisinger, Representative Ron Dellums, and Gloria Steinem.
