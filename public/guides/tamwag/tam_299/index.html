Abstract

New York City People's Life Fund and NYC War Tax Resistance were founding in 1971 by Sallie Marx in direct response to the Vietnam War. The organizations provide information, support and counseling to New Yorkers considering war tax resistance and to those involved with war tax resistance, as well as channeling resisted war tax money and contributions to communities that are underserved by government. The collection contains correspondence, pamphlets and flyers, bulletins, newsletters, grant applications and legal documents.

Immediate Source of Acquisition

Donated by Sallie Marx, Co-administrator ofthe New York City People's Life Fund and New York City War Tax Resistance in 2004; additional materials were donated by Joanie Hieger Zosike, executor of the estate of Sallie Marx, in 2017. The accession numbers associated with these gifts are 2004.023 and 2017.017.

Historical/Biographical Note

War tax resistance is a diverse movement of people who refuse to pay part or all of their federal income tax, and/or their phone tax, which is historically associated with war. New York City War Tax Resistance and the New York City People's Life Fund were founded in 1971 by Sallie Marx in direct response to the Vietnam War. The New York City War Tax Resistance provides information, support and counseling to New Yorkers considering war tax resistance and to those involved with war tax resistance. The New York City People's Life Fund channels resisted war tax money and contributions to communities that are underserved by government.

Scope and Content Note

This collection contains materials related to the New York City People's Life Fund (NYC PLF), the New York City War Tax Resistance (NYC WTR), and the National War Tax Resistance Coordinating Committee (NWTRCC). The collection contains correspondence, pamphlets and flyers, bulletins, newsletters, grant applications and legal documents. There are a substantial number of copies of the bulletin "Network News," published by the National War Tax Resistance Coordinating Committee, and "Tax Talk," published by War Tax Resistance. There is also a small amount of material produced by other peace, disarmament and anti-nuclear organizations.

Three boxes of materials added to the collection in 2017 include NYC PLF and NYC WTR files of Sallie Marx. This accretion contains People's Life Fund internal notes, correspondence, reports, and resolutions; National War Tax Resistance Coordinating Committee (NWTRCC) announcements, memos, and contact lists; and war tax resistance handbooks and pamphlets. It also contains files on tax court (1970-1981), as well as clippings, flyers, and documents from tax protests (1982-2002).
