Immediate Source of Acquisition

Historical/Biographical Note

Roland Politi is an Italian-born anarchist and artist who was involved in the 1980s squatters' rights movement in New York City's East Village which culminated in Tompkins Square Park Police Riot of August 1988.

Scope and Contents

The collection contains newspaper clippings, subject files, including eviction notices, documents relating to the New York City Community Board, and commemorative t-shirts.
