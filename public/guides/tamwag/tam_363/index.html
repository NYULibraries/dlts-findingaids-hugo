Historical/Biographical Note

Fly is a visual artist who was involved in the 1980s squatters' rights movement in New York City's East Village which culminated in the Tompkins Square Park Police Riot of August 1988.

Scope and Contents

The collection consists of photographs, a scrapbook that contains clippings, artwork and photographs, and copies of zines, including Dog Days and Slug and Lettuce.
